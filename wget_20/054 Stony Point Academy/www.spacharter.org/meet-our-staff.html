Stony Point Academy is led by professional and resourceful staff dedicated to the education and safety of your children. Our Principal strives to achieve higher levels of academic excellence by building meaningful and effective relationships between students, teachers, staff and administrators.

lkatimbang@busd.org
Lisa Katimbang
Principal

Ms. Katimbang was born and raised in Southern California.  While in college, she also served in the U.S. Army Reserves and served overseas in the Middle East for 15 months.  She first started her career in education in the Garden Grove Unified School District in Orange County, CA teaching grades K-6.  Since then, she has taught 6th grade in the San Jose area, then taught 7th and 8th grade History and Science for an Early College High School program.  Last year, she served as Academic Dean of her previous school site.  She is excited to be apart of Stony Point Academy community and looks forward to working with the students and their families in the upcoming years.
akeegan@busd.org
 Alice Keegan 
7th and 8th Grade Math 

Mrs. Keegan comes to Stony Point Charter School with a wealth of experience with students of all ages. She has spent many years tutoring and mentoring students from elementary school through college. As a homeschool parent, she educated her two sons from kindergarten to college. Recently credentialed for K-8, she also holds additional credentials in math and science. 

Making her home in Cotati, Mrs. Keegan resides with her husband and youngest son. In addition to her work at our school, she teaches and tutors middle school and high school students with special needs. When she is not working, she enjoys time with her family.

jstolze@busd.org
Julie Stolze
HS Humanities
​
Julie Stolze has been teaching middle/high school students for over eighteen years-the past eight of which were at an innovative early college high school in North Carolina. She has a passion for teaching the humanities and facilitating student discovery of connections within the human experience. Mrs. Stolze has presented at local, state, and national professional development conferences, also serving as an adjunct instructor in higher education in both California and Kansas. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Spanish from Otterbein College and a Master of Arts degree in Teaching from Chapman University. Julie Stolze is board certified in Early Adolescence Language Arts through the National Board for the Professional Teaching Standards.

7th and 8th Grade Science

Mr. Cogorno was born and raised in Sonoma County, growing up in Windsor. He has a Bachelor's in Biological Sciences from San Jose State University and multiple teaching credentials from Sonoma State University. He has worked in youth development for the past six years; four years with the Boys and Girls Club in Healdsburg and two years at Chop's Teen Club in Santa Rosa.

Nikki Doe
Home Study Coordinator

Nikki Doe comes to Stony Point Home Study with a wide variety of experiences in the Home Study community.  After graduating from Sonoma State University with her Multiple Subject Credential and teaching for several years, she started homeschooling when her daughter was in the first grade.  She is in the 13th and final year of homeschooling her own two children.  She has been an Independent Study Coordinator, a tutor, and taught in public school, private school, and several home schooling co-ops in the community.  Her passion is to give back to the community that has supported her in so many ways over her homeschooling journey.
