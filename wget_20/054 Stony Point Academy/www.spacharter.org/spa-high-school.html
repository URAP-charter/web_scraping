Mission and Vision
Stony Point Academy is committed to provide a rigorous preparatory program that ensures all students are ready for successful post-secondary pathway. This includes an academically rich curriculum with rigorous content. It includes an expectation that students will apply their knowledge through higher-order skills, and will develop the habits of mind and character traits known to support personal standards within a student-centered environment that emphasizes the Common Core Anchor Standards for College and Career Readiness (CCR) across all content areas at every grade level. It is with this preparation that students will become contributing members of the local and global communities.

​​Dear Stony Point Academy Parents,
I am writing this letter to share some exciting news with you about Stony Point Academy regarding our facilities and our governance.
In April 2015, the Bellevue Union School District School Board approved and adopted Stony Point Academy to be a part of the school district as the first middle and high school in the district.  Since we have officially become members of, and will be governed by, the Bellevue Union School District, I am excited to announce that we will have a new location beginning with the 2016-17 school year!
The existing school has served the district, community, and the students well since 2013 on 2 separate sites.  We currently have our middle school students sharing a site with Bellevue Elementary and our High School site is located on 2665 Dutton Meadow, across the parking lot from Meadow View School.  
The middle school will move to the high school location, and both will have the same address:
2665 Dutton Meadow Santa Rosa, CA 95407
 (Next to Meadow View School)
The district worked diligently to examine different floor plans and layouts, and understands our need for flexibility and technology.  The resulting school design is going to meet our needs very well. Construction will take place in June and July, and will be complete for the first day of school on August 18th.
We are looking forward to having a single location for Stony Point Academy. If you have any further questions, please call (707) 568-7504, email lkatimbang@spacharter.org or call the District Office at (707) 542-5197.
Finally, I am very pleased to announce that both of our Summer Sessions will be located at the Santa Rosa Junior College! I will provide additional information about this soon.
Go Dolphins!
 
Lisa Katimbang, Principal
Stony Point Academy
