Why Thea Bowman Is the Best Place For Your Child to Go to School


​Thea Bowman has a 90.2% graduation rate--and we are proud of it!

87% of our graduating seniors have been accepted into post-secondary education or the military.


Our students have been accepted at colleges and universities including, but not limited to: Purdue, Indiana University, Indiana State, Valparaiso University, Central State, Tuskegee University


Our students have received more than 1.8 million dollars in college scholarships.
ISTEP+ TESTING IS JUST AROUND THE CORNER!

Part 1 of ISTEP+ will be starting the week of February 27th.  It is crucial that our scholars are in the right mindset to test. Here are some things you can do at home to help:

1) Make sure your scholar gets plenty of sleep (8-10 hours per night)

2) Make sure your scholar eats a healthy breakfast each morning

3) Have your scholar practice reading longer passages or novels for stamina building

4) Have your scholar review basic math skills (integers, fractions, decimals, etc.)

With your support, we know our scholars are going to do a fantastic job!!!!
