Mission

﻿Prestige Academy prepares young men in grades 6-8 to meet or exceed grade level expectations and to prepare students for high school, college and career readiness.

In a highly structured, achievement-oriented school culture, Prestige Academy students develop a strong academic foundation in the core subjects and the REAL values necessary for success: Respect and Responsibility, Excellence in Behavior, ﻿A﻿cademic Mastery, and Leadership. ﻿

​
As a reminder: Parents of 6th and 7th grade scholars, please make sure you enroll your scholars in another school for the 2017-2018 school year. It is important that you make sure your scholar is registered for the upcoming school year.
