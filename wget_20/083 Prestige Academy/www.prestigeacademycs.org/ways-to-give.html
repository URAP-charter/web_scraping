Ways to Bridge the Gap +

  • Make a big impact! Become a Sustaining Donor Today!

    Becoming a Sustaining Donor is an easy way to increase the power of your support and put more of your dollars into the education you support.

    As a Sustaining Donor, you will...

    • Help us save dollars in postage, paper and administrative costs.
    • Utilize an eco-friendly option by allowing us to cut down on renewal mailings.
    • Help provide PACS with a steady and reliable stream of income that allows us to plan for a sustainable future and support students who relay on scholarships.

    How it Works: Your contribution is deducted automatically from the account of your choice every month. Your membership renews every year at the same amount. You can make a change or cancel at any time.

    How to Become a Sustaining Donor:

    • Choose the amount you would like to give annually.
    • Select the payment method.
    • Complete remaining required sections of the form.

    To become a Sustaining Member now fill out the form here or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

  • ﻿Unrestricted Gift to the Annual Fund﻿
    Gifts to the Annual Fund are unrestricted and support the ongoing operational budget of the school. To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

  • ﻿Support specific Scholars﻿

    Cohort Sponsorship - $25,000
    Prestige Academy has divided all of its classrooms by cohort with each cohort named after the lead teacher’s college alma mater.  This approach inspires scholars to think about college from the moment they begin Prestige and highlights the teacher’s academic accomplishments.  Currently there are fifteen (15) cohorts: Rowan, Loyola, Villanova, Delaware, Delaware State, Washington, Georgia, Bloomsburg, Temple 1, Temple 2, Temple 3, Bethune-Cookman, Notre Dame, Wilmington, and Yale.  A gift at this level will sponsor the work in a specific cohort of your choosing.  Donors will have the opportunity to visit the classroom and meet with scholars and teachers as well as follow their progress throughout the year.  To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

    Full Student Scholarship - $5,000
    Prestige Academy has identified this to be the unfunded “cost of education”.  As with all local charter schools, Prestige Academy receives approximately $3,000 less funding per student as compared to their traditional public school counterparts.  In addition, Prestige Academy is offering in the range of $2,000 per year in extra services to help its students bridge the achievement gap.  A gift at this level will support one student for the entire year.  Donors can be matched with a specific scholar, have the opportunity to meet that scholar, and follow his progress throughout the year.  Four year scholarships and endowed scholarships are also available.  Please contact the school for more information.  To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

    Partial Student Scholarship - $3,000
    A gift at this level will support the underfunded portion of a Prestige Academy scholar.  Donors can be matched with a specific scholar, have the opportunity to meet that scholar, and follow his progress throughout the year.  To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

    High School Scholarships
    Many of our scholars rise to the challenge and earn acceptances at the local private college preparatory schools.  Unfortunately, due to financial circumstances, few of our families can afford the cost of attending these schools which can exceed $20,000 per year.  Many high schools are able to provide some financial aid to families but more often than not, the aid is not sufficient for our scholars to attend.  When this happens, we would like to provide supplemental support so our scholars, who have done what we have asked of them and more, have the opportunity to attend top tier college preparatory high schools.  Your contribution will help us create an ongoing scholarship fund. For more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

  • Arts and Music
    Prestige Academy is committed to offering a variety of Art and Music offerings.  Scholars are exposed to fine arts in the class room as well as through various field trips to local museums.  Prestige has offered individual instrumental lessons for students in guitar, violin, trumpet, French horn, and drums.  We need additional support to keep these programs operating.  Prestige would like to purchase more instruments and retain a part-time music instructor.  For more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

  • Experience Gap

    College Trips ($2,500)
    We believe our scholars should be focused on attending college from the moment they begin school.  Unfortunately, many of our scholars do not come from families or communities that have placed the same expectations or priorities on them.  Many of our scholars have never set foot on a college campus.  We inspire them to make college a goal and prepare them to make it a reality.  In doing so, we take our students on a college tour at the end of each year.  This trip builds community and camaraderie like any year-end field trip would.  But more than anything, this trip inspires our scholars to dream and work towards attending and graduating from college.    Currently, less than 2% of African American boys enroll in college.  Prestige Academy and trips like these will reverse that trend.  To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

    Museums ($200 - $2,500)
    It is our desire to expose our scholars to the cultural riches that exist in our geographic area.  Our central location affords us the opportunity to visit Washington, D.C. and Baltimore to the south, New York City and Philadelphia, PA to the north, Lancaster and Gettysburg to the west as well as the rich cultural and artistic offerings in our own community.   Each year we incorporate specific trips into the curriculum to give our scholars cultural experiences they need and deserve.  Many of our scholars have never been out of Wilmington.  Exposure to the larger world is a primary component to bridging the achievement gap in learning.  To give now click Donate Now or for more information email development@pa.k12.de.us.

  • Books and Supplies

    Classroom Supplies
    Individual classroom needs vary.  We invite you to visit and take a tour of our classrooms and each cohort’s specialized bulletin board.  For more information please email development@pa.k12.de.us.

    Reading Books
    We have placed a premium on reading at Prestige Academy.  Each scholar is expected to read at least 60 minutes every day.  As a result, Prestige scholars read a lot of books and quickly develop a love for reading.  Today our challenge is keeping the quantity and variety of books our scholars need and want.   For more information on giving books or supporting the reading program please email volunteer@pa.k12.de.us.

  • Volunteering
    Prestige Academy relies heavily on a regular core of committed volunteers.  But, we always need reinforcements.   Our primary volunteer needs are in the following areas: Tutoring (specifically in math), classroom assistance, administrative support, leadership – board or committee work.  For more information, to sign up, or to receive a list of current volunteer needs please email volunteer@pa.k12.de.us.
