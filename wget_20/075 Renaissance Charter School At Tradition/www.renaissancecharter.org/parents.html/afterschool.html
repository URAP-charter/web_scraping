K-4 Afterschool Program

K-4 After School is a child-care program available to all our parents for children through the fourth grade at a cost of $7.00 per hour for the first child enrolled or $90 per week for full-time enrollment. Siblings are billed at half-price. We would also appreciate any donations that could be make to help us purchase art supplies for the afterschool program. Eligibility for scholarships are based on financial need--applications are available from Maria Banol at 718-803-0060, ext. 110.

Download the K-4 Application: K-4 Afterschool Application.

Every day we offer homework help, arts, book-time and free-play. On Fridays we offer movies.

Download the daily schedule here: K-4 Schedule

IN ADDITION: Performing Arts Conservatory of New York (PACONY) will provide private, on-site music lessons for a fee. Download the request form HERE.
