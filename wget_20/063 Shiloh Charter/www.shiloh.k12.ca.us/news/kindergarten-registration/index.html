  • Kindergarten Registration

We are now registering incoming Kindergarten students for the 2017-2018 school year. Registration will be held on Friday, February, 2017. Registration will take place here at school. If you have a child who will be 5 years old before September 1st, 2017, they are eligible to enroll in Kindergarten. Students who turn 5 years old between September 2nd and December 2nd may enroll in Transitional Kindergarten (TK). Please call the office for an appointment at 522-2261.
