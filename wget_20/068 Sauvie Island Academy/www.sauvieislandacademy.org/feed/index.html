]]> Important Schedule Changes! http://www.sauvieislandacademy.org/important-schedule-changes/ Mon, 30 Jan 2017 01:13:10 +0000 http://www.sauvieislandacademy.org/?p=723 The SIA Board met to discuss a plan for how best to recover the lost instructional minutes as a result of missed days due to the inclement weather. After weighing the benefits and drawbacks of a variety of options, the SIA Board voted last night and approved the following plan:

  1. Add Additional Minutes:
    1. Beginning Monday, Feb. 6th SIA will start 10 minutes earlier everyday
  1. Add Back Days:
    1. Make Jan. 27th a student school day, and move the semester end date to Feb. 16th
    2. Make April 13th a student school day, and remove the Teacher Work Day
    3. Make April 14th AND April 17th student school days, and remove the Parent Conference Days.
    4. Make June 15th a full student day, instead of a half day and move the last day of school
    5. Make June 16th the last day of school and make it a full student day

An updated calendar is available here. Please scroll all the way to the bottom of this post to view all of the information.

 

Beginning Feb 6, 2017:

SIA Start Times

Monday Late-Start (9:25AM-3:05PM)

Tues-Friday (8:25AM-3:05PM)

Classrooms will open 5 minutes prior to the start time.

Busing Changes

In order to accommodate the new start time, beginning Feb 6th the NEW drop off time at the bus hub at the Scappoose Middle School will be:

Monday Late-Start (8:45AM-9:00AM)

Tues-Friday (7:45AM-8:00AM)

All SIA Shuttle Buses will depart by 8:05AM (9:05AM on Monday Late-Starts)

All AM Island bus pickup times will be adjusted to 10 minutes earlier.
