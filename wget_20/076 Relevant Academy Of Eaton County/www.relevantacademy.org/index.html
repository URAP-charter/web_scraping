Program Description

THE RELEVANT ACADEMY is a tuition-free public school academy.  We provide a need to serve a specific population of learners who have disengaged from traditional school, yet are willing to try something innovative and new. This learning experience is for high school aged students who are ready to start again and redefine themselves.

WE BELIEVE that all people are natural learners, redefining school will redefine attitudes, and with enough support, all types of learners can achieve their goals.
