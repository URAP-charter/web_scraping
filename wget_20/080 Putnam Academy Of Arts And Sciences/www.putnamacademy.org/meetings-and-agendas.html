Regular meetings of the Governing Board shall
generally be held on the second Thursday of each month at 4:30 pm in the
cafeteria of the school, located at 113 Putnam County Blvd., East  Palatka.
Special meetings will be announced in advance with times and locations.

Parents and other members of the community are invited to join
us for these meetings to offer input or to keep informed.

 2014
