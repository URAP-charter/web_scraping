The SEED School of Washington, D.C.

The nation's first public, charter, college-preparatory boarding school

Our mission is to provide an outstanding, intensive educational program that prepares children, both academically and socially, for success in college and beyond.

Enriching Academics

Our college-preparatory environment challenges all students in small classroom settings and with individualized instruction rooted in our core values. Learn about our model here →

24-HOUR LEARNING

With our boarding program, we are not limited by the confines of a classroom. We encourage students to grow through their life experiences as well as their academic experiences. Learn More →

College support

SEED graduates are blazing the trail towards college graduation with an army of advocates at their side. Learn more about our college transition and success program here →
