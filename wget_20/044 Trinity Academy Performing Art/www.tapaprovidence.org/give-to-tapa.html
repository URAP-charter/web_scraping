ANNUAL APPEAL SUMMER 2016
Dear Friends of TAPA, 

We did it! The Rhode Island Board of Education (RIDE) has unanimously approved our charter renewal for another five years! TAPA offers the only arts-integrated education in Providence, where our students learn that the arts - dance, film, music and theatre - are inextricably woven into the fabric of their education. I want to share the powerful words of a thankful TAPA mom who sums up the TAPA experience perfectly: 
"﻿My daughter is a bright, creative student; with the help of TAPA she is slowly blossoming into a true artist. From an academic standpoint, TAPA challenges my daughter to work hard and achieve academic success at the highest levels she can. My daughter is more of a visual and auditory learner, so learning through dance, music, and other art forms has created a much better academic experience for her. My family and I always feel welcome at TAPA, whether it's to discuss a concern about my daughter, volunteering my time at the school, or just stopping by to say hello. The integration of arts and academics showcases the success of students that may not otherwise excel in a normal classroom setting. My daughter is certainly an example of that, and I know she will continue to grow and achieve greatness at TAPA.﻿"
Your generous contribution will help us continue to educate, inspire and engage our students. 
Thank you for supporting our TAPA Stars!
When you participate in our annual fund, TAPA's filmmakers, dancers, musicians, actors and theatre technicians will continue to reach for the stars. A gift of any size is meaningful and appreciated. Thank you for your support of our innovative programs and dynamic student body. 

"If I had gone to another school, I never would have become a filmmaker. My dream is to bring a little bit of Hollywood to Rhode Island, so I can bring a little bit of Rhode Island to Hollywood!" - Preston 

  • $5,000 can provide industry-standard filmmaking equipment. 
  • $2,500 can purchase cutting-edge scientific equipment and technology. 
  • $1,000 can provide musical instruments and travel cases. 
  • $500 can send a class on a creative and educational field trip. 
  • $250 can purchase art supplies and classroom equipment. 

All donations are greatly appreciated. 

Gratefully,
Nanci DeRobbio
Head of School
