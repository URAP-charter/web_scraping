Admissions

TAPA is a 7 - 12 public charter school for students living in the city of Providence with a passion for the performing arts.
Students are admitted to TAPA by lottery. Families wishing to be entered into the lottery should complete an admissions application. Applications may be completed online using the link below, at an open house or by visiting the school's main office.
We encourage all interested families to attend an open house event prior to the lottery.

***The deadline to apply for the 2017-18 school year is February 24th, 2017.***
The admissions lottery will take place March 1st, 2017. Applicant families will be notified of the results by mail. Students not admitted to TAPA via the lottery will be placed on a waiting list, which TAPA will use to fill any spots that become available throughout the school year. Applications received after the submission deadline of 2/24/17 will be placed on the waiting list, after the lottery takes place, in the order received.

For questions about admissions or wait list process, please contact Zuleika Vidal, Admissions Coordinator, (401) 644-7657, ms.vidal@tapaprovidence.org
