Since 1997, River Valley Charter School has joined students, parents,
and teachers together to foster a positive and successful learning environment.

River Valley is a small, college preparatory Junior and Senior High School that will prepare
all students for college and inspire all students to become self-motivated, competent, and lifelong learners.
