Jr High Science Teacher

Paraprofessional Educator

Position: Junior High Science Teacher

Job Type: Full Time

Position Area: Biology/Life Science, Chemistry, Earth Science, Environmental Science, Physics, Science (General), Space Science

Location: West Valley City, Utah

Start: Immediately

 

 

About the position

Mana Academy is looking for a full time Junior High Science teacher to teach Integrated Science 7, Integrated Science 8 and an elective class in a modified A/B schedule. Class sizes range from 13 to 21 students. You will be teaching in a technology rich environment, with a 1:1 technology/student ratio.

 

Candidate will be working with a diverse student body and community, so it is imperative that the candidate quickly acclimate to the school culture, actively participates in community building by creating and fostering partnerships with parents, attend all trainings and meetings. Teachers will make two home visits throughout the academic year.

 

Qualifications:

 

  • An undergraduate degree in Science, graduate degree is highly preferred
  • Current Utah teaching license (will consider ARL, or APT candidate with strong qualifications)
  • Experience in teaching highly preferred
  • Experience building relationships with students and parents
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Critical thinking and good listening skills
  • Well-developed emotional intelligence (ability to empathize/sympathize and motivate)
  • Emotional maturity and stability necessary for the specialized work involved
  • Comfort with technology, social media and learning management systems
  • Team-focused, “can-do” attitude about the achievement of goals
  • Superior organizational skills, ability to prioritize, multi-task and work independently
  • Experience working with culturally diverse communities

 

Responsibilities

  • Plan and implement curriculum for daily lessons, academic advisory and/or an elective class
  • Implement standards-based teaching methods in classroom to improve learning outcomes and meet school goals
  • Maintain data wall weekly to track student progress
  • Manage a caring, respectful, student-centered classroom
  • Work effectively with diverse student population
  • Participate meaningfully in teacher team and professional development activities

 

About Mana Academy

Mana Academy Charter School is dedicated to excellence and committed to closing the opportunity gap by nurturing life long learners who are rooted in their native cultures, and preparing them to be leaders of tomorrow.

 

Mana Academy is an equal opportunity employer. We offer a competitive benefit package to qualified employees.

 

If you are an educator with several years of experience or a teacher just starting their career and wish to teach in a unique educational environment and have a passion for science, please email your resume to HR Director, Silvia Garcia: sgarcia@themanaacademy.org
