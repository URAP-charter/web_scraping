School Year Calendars

Download the Calendar

2017-18

The Board of Education approved the 2017-18 school year calendar at its Jan. 9, 2017 meeting. The calendar can be downloaded from our website.

2018-19 calendar

A skeletal calendar has been adopted by the Board of Education for 2018-19. A complete calendar is scheduled to be presented to the school board in early 2018.

2016-17

The 2016-2017 school calendar (download the preschool calendar here) was approved by the Board of Education on January 25, 2016. The calendar was revised on April 12, 2016, to adjust the scheduled for calamity make-up days. With the revision:

  • The district’s first calamity day will be made up on President’s Day, Feb. 20, 2017.
  • The second, third, and fourth calamity days will be made up with blizzard bags.
  • The fifth calamity day will be made up April 17, 2017.
  • Note that the junior high schools are on trimesters starting in 2016-17. The first trimester ends Nov. 11 (interim grades: Sept. 30), the second trimester ends Feb. 17 (interim grades: Jan. 6), and the third trimester concludes at the end of the school year (interim grades: April 7).
