About Our PTO

Welcome!

Dear Parents and Families,

The Pickerington Elementary School Parent Teacher Organization (PTO) would like to welcome you to our official web page. For those of you who are unfamiliar with us or new to our school, we are a volunteer organization made up of parents, teachers, administrators, and support staff who are all dedicated to the successful education of our children. The Pickerington Elementary PTO works hard to help make the school year both enjoyable and exciting for students, teachers, and parents.

The PES PTO would like to extend a warm welcome to all new and returning families to the 2016-2017 school year. Working together we can truly make a difference at Pickerington Elementary. Every positive imprint we make on our children can go a long way. If you are able to give some time to help co-chair an event or volunteer in the school, please join us! Many hands make the job easier! We will send home a PTO packet with the children at the beginning of the school year, where you will get the opportunity to join our PTO and sign up to volunteer for certain committees. In addition, the PTO will meet on the third Wednesday of each month at 7pm at our school. We invite and encourage you to attend as the school and PTO will benefit from hearing your ideas, and you will be better informed about all the past and upcoming events at the school.

Some of the past events our PTO has made possible are our Back to School Ice Cream
event, STEM day, Father/Daughter fun night, Mother/Son bowling, Movie Nights, Book Fairs, field trips, and Super Games to name a few. The PTO also provides funding to the teachers and other staff members to enhance their learning curriculum. We in the PTO believe that it is the ultimate importance that we do everything we can to enhance the learning environment of our children as they truly are our future. It will take all of us working together to ensure that this happens.

We look forward to getting to know you!
