Rachael Moore

Hello Pickerington families, I am the shared art teacher between Pickerington and Fairfield Elementary Schools. I believe that art has a special role in helping students make sense and meaning of their understandings, so I enjoy facilitating the communication of their ideas in ways that are both conceptually and aesthetically successful.

Education

PreK-12 Ohio Licensed Visual Art Teacher
Master of Arts, The Ohio State University
Bachelor of Art Education, The Ohio State University, Summa Cum Laude

Additional Information

A little about me personally - I was born and raised in Kentucky, moved to the Newark, Ohio area and graduated from Lakewood High School in 2004, and earned my Bachelor’s of Art Education Degree in 2008 from The Ohio State University. From there, I ironically student taught in PLSD during the 2008-09 school year with Mary Sheridan at Tussing Elementary. After obtaining licensure in 2009, I went on to earn my Master’s Degree in 2010, while simultaneously long-term substitute teaching in the arts. Then from 2011 until 2014, I worked with a variety of both students, teachers, and non-profit organizations as an art-integration specialist implementing arts-based interdisciplinary programming across the curriculum. Beginning in 2014, I became an art teacher in this wonderful district and have been soaking it up ever since. When I’m not having a blast with your students in the art room, I enjoy things like nature and the outdoors, health and fitness, decorating and design, friends and family, and anything black and white chevron ;P Just ask your kiddos!
