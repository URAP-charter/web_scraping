Visitors

We ask all visitors to sign in and obtain a visitors pass to be in the school or on the playground.

Parents are asked to respect the teacher’s responsibility to provide instruction from 9;15-4:05 p.m. We try to limit the number of classroom interruptions during the school day.

A conference is an ideal way for parents and teacher to get to know one another so the best interest of your child is realized.  Visiting in the classroom is limited and must be scheduled in advance with the teacher.

Dismissal

We ask that your child follow the same routine daily going home to avoid any confusion at the end of the day.  One of the most difficult things for us to do is manage all the last minute changes on how students are to go home.  If there needs to be a change, please send a note to your child’s teacher for any changes to end of day dismissal.  If an emergency arises, contact the school office before 2:00 so we may inform the teacher of change.

Also, please send in a note for early dismissals.

2017/2018 School Registration Dates

If you have a child who will be enrolling for Kindergarten for school year 2017/2018, registration opens on March 16.  On that date you can go to www.pickerington.k12.oh.us and look for I Want To enroll my student.  Follow the directions for online registration.

New Students for Grades First-Twelve can start registration process on May 1, 2017.  If your child is already attending PLSD schools, they will automatically be enrolled for next school year.

 

Open Enrollment (IntraDistrict Building Transfer) for 2017/2018

IntraBuilding Transfer (OE) is only in effect for one school year and there is no guarantee of approval for your child in future years.  You are required to complete an application each year for your child to be considered for the Open Enrollment program. The application for the 2017-2018 school year must be completed and on file with the district by March 30, 2017 as per district policy.

2017-2018 Classroom Placement Recommendations

When making decisions about classroom placement, many variables are considered.  If you desire, you may complete a questionnaire to provide us with information regarding your child.  The completed form should be returned to the school office no later than April 28.

 

Weather Delays

Occasionally, Pickerington Schools may determine it is appropriate to close or delay school start times based on inclement weather or other reasons.

These decisions are made for the safety of our students. We understand that these decisions have a big effect on families as they plan their day, and now is an excellent time to establish your family emergency plan for such potential closings.

Phone and email alerts

Pickerington Schools will announce emergency closing and delay information through pre-recorded telephone calls to current parents/guardians telephone numbers on file with the district.

The district also sends email messages about any delays or closures via this notification system.

It is important to keep student contact information and contact preferences current by logging into the Infinite Campus Parent Portal and updating your contact information.
