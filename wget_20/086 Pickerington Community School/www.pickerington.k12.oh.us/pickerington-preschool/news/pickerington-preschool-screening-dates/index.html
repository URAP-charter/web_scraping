Pickerington Preschool Screening dates

The Pickerington Preschool screenings for the 17-18 school year will be held on Thursday, February 23, 2017 or Friday, February 24, 2016 at Tussing Elementary School.

We will be screening 3 & 4 year old children to be typical models for Pickerington Local Schools Special Needs Classrooms for the 17-18 school year. 

Who should be screened?

  • Children 3 & 4 year olds not attending kindergarten
  • Children that have excellent communication skills.
  • Children who are outgoing
  • Children that show empathy for others
  • Children who have cooperative play skills
  • Children who are LEADERS

Screenings will include: Speech and Language Skills, Gross Motor Skills, Fine Motor Skills and Cognitive Skills

To ensure space for next year, Complete the online application at:

www.pickerington.k12.oh.us

Click on our schools –select preschool

Click on our school — select Applying to Pickerington Preschool

Open the Preschool Application section

Choose 17-18 preschool application

Once this is submitted, Tammy Pruden or Sherri Dittoe will contact you with a date and time for your screening.

Please see the screening flyer 2017 for more information including: cost, transportation and contact information for questions.
