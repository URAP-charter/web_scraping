Back to School 2016

It might be summer, but here in Pickerington Schools we're already thinking about students coming back to school for fall! From supply lists to student handbooks, from school menus to easy ways to update your family's information online, this page is your one-stop shop with all the information you'll need to get your kids ready for school!

Information for Preschool at Tussing Elementary

Meet the Teacher

On Friday, Aug. 19, we invite preschool students to come and visit their new classrooms! This is a great time for students to meet their teacher an see their classroom for the 2016-17 school year.

The classrooms will be open for one hour windows for you and your child to stop by. Please follow the times below:

  • AM Class: 9:30-10:30 a.m.
  • PM Class: 1:30-2:30 p.m.

This is a great time to bring in your child’s supplies, change of clothes, and paperwork before their first day of school.

See you soon!

Preschool Parent Information Night (Tussing)

Time: 6 p.m.

Date: Thursday, August 18

Where: Tussing Elementary

What: This information night will include:

  • A welcome presentation by the preschool supervisor
  • Information presented by the school nurse
  • Break-apart meetings in your child’s classroom to meet the teacher and receive classroom information

Please bring all of your child’s completed school and medical forms, as well as all supplies, diapers, etc.

Please note: This is a parent presentation only. Childcare will not be provided. Your child will have the chance to see the classroom and meet their teacher at the Friday, Aug. 19, “Meet the Teacher” event described above.

School Supply Lists (Tussing)

All students must bring diapers and baby wipes, if needed

Boys should bring:

  • One box of Crayola Classic 10-pack of markers
  • One package of character stickers (such as Cars, Ninja Turtles, My Little Pony, Disney characters)
  • Three glue sticks
  • Hand sanitizer

Girls should bring:

  • One box of Crayola crayons
  • One package of character stickers (such as Cars, Ninja Turtles, My Little Pony, Disney characters)
  • Three glue sticks
  • Dry erase markers
