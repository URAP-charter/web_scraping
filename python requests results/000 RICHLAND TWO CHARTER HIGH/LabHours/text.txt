      <h1>
       Lab Hours
      </h1>
      <p>
       The Richland Two Charter High School is open for student use Monday through Thursday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM and Friday until 3:00 PM.
       <br/>
       <strong>
        During Evening Hours, please keep in mind
       </strong>
       :
      </p>
      <div>
       <ul>
        <li>
         The lab will close at 7:10 PM if no students have arrived by that time.
        </li>
        <li>
         Please arrive no later than 6:00 PM to take quizzes.
        </li>
        <li>
         Students will not be permitted to log on to a computer after 7:10 PM (this allows at least thirty 30 minutes to complete any work before having to log out by 7:45 PM).
        </li>
        <li>
         The latest start time for a course exam is 6:45 PM (this allows a one-hour minimum per exam session as the length of exams varies from course to course).
        </li>
        <li>
         Monday through Thursday, all computers must be shut down by 7:45 PM.
        </li>
        <li>
         Fridays, all computers must be shut down by 2:45 PM.
        </li>
        <li>
         Students working from home and have questions are encouraged call the computer lab at 803-419-1348. Please note that the lab in use may differ from night to night according to which staffer is attending. See the list below.
        </li>
       </ul>
      </div>
