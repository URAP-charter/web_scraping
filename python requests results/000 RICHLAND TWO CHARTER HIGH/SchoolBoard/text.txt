      <h3>
       2016-2017 Election Results:
      </h3>
      <p>
       Sue Dillon, Audrey Ratchford, and Margaret Walden have been elected to serve two-year terms.
       <br/>
       Brenda Mack-Foxworth was appointed to serve a 2-year term.
       <br/>
       Ballots were due Tuesday, October 18 @ 4pm.
      </p>
      <hr/>
      <h3>
       School Board Meeting Dates
      </h3>
      <h4>
       Richland Two Charter High School 2016-2017 School Board Meeting Dates
      </h4>
      <p>
       Board meetings are usually the 3rd Monday evening of each month at 6:00 PM at the Richland Two Charter High School at Anna Boyd. The October meeting is on Thursday. December and January meetings are on the 2nd Monday due to scheduling conflicts.
      </p>

       <li>
        August 15, 2016
       </li>
       <li>
        September 19, 2016
       </li>
       <li>
        October 20, 2016 (Thursday)
       </li>
       <li>
        November 21, 2015
       </li>
       <li>
        December 12, 2016 (2nd Monday; 3rd Monday is a holiday)
       </li>
       <li>
        January 9, 2017 (2nd Monday, 3rd Monday is a holiday)
       </li>

        February 20, 2017
       </li>
