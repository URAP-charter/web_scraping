  • Our Mission

    GALS is the first and only all-girls charter school in Colorado.  We focus on a movement-based approach to education and instill confidence and self-advocacy in our students.


  • Thank You!

    I look around this room and think of all the people here, how different we are, how powerful we are, how beautiful we are, how smart we are, and yet we are the same. We are women, and together we can make a difference in how we are viewed by the world. Thank you GALS for helping me see this."

    - 6th grader at GALS Denver
