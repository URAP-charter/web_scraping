iPad Deployment Information

  • $75 Technology Fee must be paid before student picks up the iPad on 8/15.
  • Students do NOT need an Apple ID. iPads have a managed Apple ID assigned from the school.
  • If a student/parent is unable to come to the initial deployment on 8/15, the student can pick up their iPad on 8/16 during the morning orientation. A parent does not need to be present for a student to pick up their iPad; however, parents must pay the $75 technology fee prior to orientation.
  • Students will receive an iPad, a cover, a charger, and a cord at deployment.
  • Tech AUPs and iPad AUPs will be reviewed and signed by students during student iLicense training and then sent home for parents to read and sign.
  • Students will receive their iLicense after participating in either the new or refresher iLicense training sessions.
  • Students choosing to use their own personal iPad must still pay the $75 technology fee and will be required to clear their iPad of their personal apps and agree to have their iPad managed by the school for the school year.
  • If a student chooses to use their own personal iPad, they will need to bring their iPad to the tech ofﬁce from 9:00-12:00 on 8/15, to have their iPad wiped and the school proﬁle installed.
