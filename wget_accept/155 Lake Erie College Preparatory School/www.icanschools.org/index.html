Mission & Vision

The mission of I CAN SCHOOLS is to prepare students for a college preparatory high school that will ensure success and graduation from a four-year college.
 
Our vision creates a culture of high expectations for behavior and academics through rigorous, research-based curricula and a focus on achievement. Each of our scholars is expected to attend a college preparatory high school, to gain acceptance to and graduate from a four-year college, and to positively affect change in their local community while making a contribution to our global society.
