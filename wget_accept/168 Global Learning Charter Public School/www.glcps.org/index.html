Did you know?

Charter public schools enroll twice the percentage of poor and minority students as district schools statewide. At Global Learning Charter Public School, 7.9% of students are English learners, a number that has doubled each year fo the past three years, and that trend is expected to continue. The state average of English learners is 9%. The percentage of students with disabilities at GLCPS is 14%, only 3% lower than the statewide average.
