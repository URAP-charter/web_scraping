Welcome to Sage Academy!

Sage Academy Charter School is a Tuition-Free School of Choice chartered by the State of Arizona Board of Education.

Our vision is to positively impact students, parents, and the community by applying a traditional approach to a solid and sound education system for our children. Nothing is more important than giving a child the best education available.

Our mission at Sage Academy is to challenge each student to learn, grow and excel in character, knowledge, wisdom and life.
