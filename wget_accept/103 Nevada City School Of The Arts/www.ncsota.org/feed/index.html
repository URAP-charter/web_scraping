Great Condominium in Mammoth Lakes – Three days and two nights.

The condominium is in the Sunrise at Mammoth development located in the Scenic Mammoth Meadow area. It sleeps up to six people with one bedroom, a loft, and two full baths. The condominium is fully furnished and you can enjoy a great view of the Sherwins – and Mammoth Mountain. It is located close to Mammoth Creek and Mammoth Creek Park for kid activities.

 

You can easily walk to bus routes for rides to the lifts and into town. The winner needs to contact the owners for available dates – Hugh & Katie Coffin – hcoffin@cox.net or (949) 497-1559. The condominium will not be available for the following holidays: Memorial Day, Fourth of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas and New Year. Use is subject to prior booking.

 

The winning bid must be claimed within one year.

2 Bedroom, 1.5 bath, 2 story on 3 acres with 365 degree views

View all details

This is a very clean and cozy custom built home, in a quiet and quaint location between Victor and Driggs. From the kitchen and back yard, you have a panoramic view of the Tetons. It is a two-story home with a wood burning stove and central heat. The two bedrooms and full bath are upstairs, and the kitchen, living room and half bath are downstairs. One bedroom has a queen size bed, the other has a queen size bed and 2 twin beds. On the top of the stairs, there is a wide landing with a couch that unfolds to sleep an extra adult or child. The kitchen is more than fully equipped with brand new kitchen apparatuses. There is a washer and dryer for your convenience. Outside, there is a swing set for children, a large barn to store your vehicle and wood, and a corral for 2 horses. Both dogs and horses are allowed upon approval of owner, for an extra pet fee.
This house is near the foothills of the Teton Range, minutes away from many Targhee National Forest trailheads and creeks. Jackson Hole is a mere 25 miles away, Grand Targhee ski resort is 25 minutes away, Yellowstone Park is a little over an hour away and the Grand Teton National Park is fifty minutes away. This home is the perfect base camp for countless adventures in the area, summer and winter alike!

Nancy Fisher is a local, award-winning doll artist. This piece, entitled “Millie the Mermaid and Fritz the Fish”, is of her own original design. Millie is handmade using polymer clay for her head, body, arms and hands over a wire armature, with a felted tail covered with fabric. Fritz has a styrofoam core covered in a batik fabric with inset eyes and fabric fins. This piece was included in the Petaluma Art Center’s “Journeys Through Light & Dark: Dolls as Tellers of Stories” exhibit in 2016 and is approximately 27″ tall.

(Please note that Millie’s hand sustained a small crack during exhibit – on her right pinky finger.)
