Union logo with website 2013[/caption]Celebrating their 150th Anniversary in 2014, The Union is one of the oldest daily newspapers in California. Founded in 1864, as the Civil War raged and people in Nevada County were divided. The Union made its stand clear: “For The Union, One and Inseparable.” Those words have appeared on its front page every day since then. Today, in addition to providing the newspaper six days a week, they are online (www.theunion.com), sponsor many non-profit organizations, produce events, and provide high-quality commercial printing at discounted rates to our community.

 

Thank you The Union! We appreciate your support.
