These drawings were created in the fall of 2015 art class. Students used a photographic reference of their choice and first drew a gesture of the animal, then layered the shapes, values, colors and textures using vine charcoal & soft pastel. The writing on the card represents what the students feel the animal might be thinking.

Please fill out Animal Notecards Order Form and deliver to the front office or art room 101, Middle School. All card sets are stored in the art room and will be held for pick – up there. Examples of the cards are in a binder in the front office, Middle School campus. All proceeds from the sale of these cards will be used to purchase materials for art classes. Please pay in cash only & place this form and cash in an envelope. No checks, please.  Cards are not sold individually.

Interstellar is the web application that makes Math Madness possible. It features live, team-based, online academic competition over multiple choice and open entry questions. With it, an educator can create a virtual team of students and then arrange competitions for that team by dividing it into multiple teams that compete with each other, or by inviting another team in the Interstellar Network to a match. We call this brand of competition Pick-Up Play. Alternatively, an educator can enter his/her team into a cycle of regularly occurring contests organized by Interstellar. We call this brand of competition League Play. The Coral Math Team played four matches, and then qualified for the second round.
