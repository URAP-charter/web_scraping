e-Institute is a FREE charter school that offers an excellent alternative opportunity for high school completion. Our computer based programs are aligned with the Arizona State Standards and offer the latest in educational assessment, guided practice, enhancement and remedial course studies. In addition, we also offer classes in the traditional setting such as; AIMS reading, writing, and math prep courses.

We provide a self-pace setting that’s ideal for students who want to speed up their graduation date or catch up on credits. Our flexible schedule makes it convenient for those students who have jobs or other responsibilities. We have two-four hour sessions five days a week.

Our e-Institute Charter High Schools conveniently serve 6 valley locations. For all locations, call (602) 870-2000.
