Quality Education
At C.M. Grant Leadership Academy, our strong back-to-basics curriculum ensures that every child receives a solid foundation for learning and the first steps toward a lifetime of success. Focusing on math, science, English language arts and social studies, our program provides every student the building blocks they need to be successful throughout his or her school years. Teachers at C.M. Grant are fully certified, highly qualified and dedicated to providing individualized instruction to every child at the academy.

Our curriculum is built on academic rigor and teaches to all learning styles. Our academy uses a quality, proven curriculum promotes mastery of all core subjects: math, English language arts, social studies and science. Employing various learning styles and teaching strategies, the curriculum provides the foundation for expanded learning opportunities and enables all students to achieve academic success.
