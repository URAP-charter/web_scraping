On this page, you will find everything you need to know to have your child ready for kindergarten. For more information, or to schedule a visit, please contact the school.

In order for your child to be completely enrolled in Kindergarten, the following documents MUST be provided before your child can begin:
  1. Copy of birth certificate
  2. Copy of social security card
  3. Copy of immunization records
  4. Copy of custody and/or adoption paperwork (if applicable)
  5. Proof of residency (MUST be a current utility bill, signed lease agreement or notarized residency affidavit; NO EXCEPTIONS!!)
