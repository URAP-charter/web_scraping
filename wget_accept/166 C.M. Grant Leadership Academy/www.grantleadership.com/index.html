BOOST Your Child's Education

Introducing the "Boost" program by C.M. Grant Leadership Academy.

If your child is behind their current grade level in reading or math, our program is designed to get them caught up and back on track. Students receive free additional services to strengthen the areas in which they are struggling.

Don't let your child fall behind or get held back. At C.M. Grant, we care about your child's success.

To learn more about the "Boost" program and how C.M. Grant Leadership Academy can help your child, contact us today.
