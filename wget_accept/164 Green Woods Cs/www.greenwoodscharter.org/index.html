4th Grade Students Earn Award for Work to Protect Watershed
Students in our 4th grade received the Schuylkill Drinking Water Award from the Schuylkill Action Network as part of their yearlong efforts to protect the local watershed. Their project included learning about the waterways in our region, raising trout in the classroom, and marking storm drains in the neighborhood to help raise awareness about waterways in our area. TEACHERS AS STUDENTS: THE EIC MODEL IN ACTION!
Green Woods teaching staff is always learning. One way we prepared for the new school year was by stepping outside the classroom walls and into the great outdoors. In 2014, our school was designated as a National Model for teaching the EIC Program. EIC stands for using the Environment as an Integrating Context and, for more than a decade, the Green Woods teachers have been developing our own EIC curriculum, building supportive partnerships, and honing our skills to bring rich, cross-curricular learning experiences to our K-8 students.

GREEN WOODS COMPREHENSIVE PLAN

The Pennsylvania Department of Education requires GWCS to complete a strategic plan every six years. The Comprehensive Plan indicates how Green Woods will address academic standards and assessment, professional education, teacher induction, special education, educational technology and student services The Plan will guide our continuous improvement efforts to identify future goals and improve educational opportunities and achievement for all of our students.

The draft version of the Comprehensive Plan for 2017 - 2020 is posted here for public comment. We invite you to read it and send Dr. Nunery any questions or comments you may have (lnunery@greenwoodscharter.org).

The finished Plan will be approved by the Board of Directors by November 30 and submitted to PDE.
