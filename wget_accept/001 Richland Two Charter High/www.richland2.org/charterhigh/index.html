Tuesday, February 21 - Military Academy Day

​

United States Senator Lindsey O. Graham is pleased to invite interested students from across South Carolina to participate in Military Academy Day 2017. This annual event, hosted by his office, allows students to learn more about applying to and attending one of our nation's prestigious service academies. Representatives from the United States Air Force Academy, United States Coast Guard Academy, United States Merchant Marine Academy, United States Military Academy, United States Naval Academy and United States Department of Defense Medical Examination Review Board will take part in the program.

Please RSVP by Tuesday, March 28, 2017, via www.lgraham.senate.gov/academydayregistration.
