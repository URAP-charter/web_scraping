Central PA Digital Learning Foundation
We are a virtual charter school that strives to create a learning culture that empowers and motivates learners in a flexible, customizable environment.


 Community Atmosphere
CPDLF provides a small-school setting in a cyber-environment.  We work very closely with our students and their Families to help ensure Success

Certified Teachers
CPDLF provides teachers for students that are PA-Certified in the core content areas.  Students at CPDLF have easy access to teachers.
 
Computer Systems Provided for School Use
CPDLF furnishes students with a computer system and printer to complete their work.  CPDLF also provides reimbursements for internet access.
 
No Tuition
CPDLF is free for all Pennsylvania Residents of school age.
 
2017-2020 Comprehensive Plan 
 The draft Comprehensive Plan for the span of school years 2017 through 2020 is on the About Us page, under the Comprehensive Plan tab.   This plan provides direction for the organization and sets goals to achieve during the period this plan covers and will be filed with the PA Department of Education on November 29, 2016.  Please review and submit any comments or suggestions to ceo@cpdlf.org by November 28, 2016.
