Principal’s Note: Steve Butcher

Dear Parents,

Welcome to Leading Edge Academy Mountain View in Queen Creek.  I invite you to explore our website as there is a wealth of information here.  In addition, I would be happy to speak with you personally and give you a tour of our campus.  Just give us a call.

As principal, my commitment is to provide a character-building community that is safe, nurturing, and encouraging.  We will continue to challenge each student academically to reach his or her highest potential.  Our staff of caring, dedicated professionals will provide academic rigor accompanied by support and encouragement to each student.

We embrace the opportunity to make a lasting impact on students’ lives and look forward to celebrating their successes with you!

Steve Butcher
Principal
