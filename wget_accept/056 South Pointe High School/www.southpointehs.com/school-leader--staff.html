Larry McGill, Jr., school leader
Larry McGill, Jr., a Phoenix resident, is the school leader of South Pointe High School in   south-central Phoenix, a tuition-free Leona Group public charter school. Mr. McGill has served as school leader since South Pointe’s inception in 2003. Prior to assuming the role of school leader at South Pointe, Mr. McGill served as school leader for three years at Leona's Desert Hills High School located in Gilbert, Arizona. Before that, he held the position of assistant school leader at Summit High School, another Leona sister school. Mr. McGill has been a member of The Leona Group family for 16 years.

Mr. McGill holds a bachelor's degree in psychology from Ottawa University and is a member of the National Association of Secondary School Principals.  He first entered the education profession as a dropout prevention specialist at Maryvale High School in Phoenix in 1995. He then served as an academic advisor at Genesis Academy in Phoenix in 1998.

As the school leader at South Pointe High, Mr. McGill serves as a leader for the faculty and staff. He ensures that his staff is Highly -Qualified and has access to resources that create a positive, risk-free learning environment. In collaboration with the planning team, Mr. McGill was instrumental in the South Pointe Learning Community’s expansion, opening of South Pointe Junior High in 2007, and South Pointe Elementary School in 2008.  Now a complete K-12 system, South Pointe is educating students with high standards and preparing them for a bright future as successful leaders in society from kindergarten through graduation.

​A Phoenix native, Mr. McGill is a role-model for the students in the South Pointe Learning Community. He prides himself on open lines of communication between the families and the staff of South Pointe. Mr. McGill is readily accessible to everyone in the community and willing to help and support them all in any way he can. The South Pointe Learning Community’s pillar is helping all students succeed, no matter their situation. Mr. McGill works tirelessly, with his staff and students, creating a culture that is rigorous enough to challenge highly motivated students so they can go on to receive scholarships and attend acclaimed colleges and universities, as well as addressing the challenges that cause some students to lose sight of the strength they can gain from a solid education. South Pointe and Mr. McGill are about creative problem-solving, working around challenges, and never giving up.
