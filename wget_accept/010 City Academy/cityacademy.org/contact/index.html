The majority of the classes are held in the city-owned Wilder Recreation Center. Classes are also held at satellite locations to strengthen communication and connections between the students, the school and the community. If you are going to take a city bus to get here, you can check the MetroTransit page, where they provide info on all city buses including schedules and maps. A very common way of arriving at City Academy is via Route 64.
