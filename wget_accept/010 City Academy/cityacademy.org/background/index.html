Background

City Academy High School is located on the East Side of St. Paul in the Wilder Recreation Center. City Academy was created in 1992, in cooperation with the City of St. Paul and Northern States Power Company under the sponsorship of St. Paul District #625 and the Minnesota Department of Education.

City Academy is Minnesota Independent School District #4000 and a Minnesota Non-profit Corporation. As of September 2000, City Academy is under the sponsorship of St. Catherine University, in St. Paul, Minnesota.
