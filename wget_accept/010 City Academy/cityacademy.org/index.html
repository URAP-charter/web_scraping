City Academy High School is a public charter school in Saint Paul, Minnesota. We were the first charter school to begin operation in the country. Our students are typically between 17 and 21 years old, and have experienced challenges in traditional academic settings. We focus on providing opportunities for our students to either attend postsecondary institutions or begin employment. Our school shares facilities with a neighborhood recreation center, and utilizes community resources as much as possible. Please contact us with any questions.
