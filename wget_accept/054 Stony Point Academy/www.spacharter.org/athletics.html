Soccer Coach

Arnaldo Salazar
​
Coach Salazar was born October 16, 1989. Raised in Sonoma County most of his life, and lived in Southern California for a short time. His family currently resides in Santa Rosa. He is the youngest of three, with two older sisters. He graduated from El Camino High in Los Angeles, CA in 2008. He played professionally with Chivas USA and it was cut short due to injuries. In 2009, Coach Salazar attended Santa Rosa Junior College. He was in a terrible car accident, which killed a friend and injured himself and two others. Coach Salazar took 2 years off from any harsh activity, including soccer, after the car accident. His mentality changed, and so did his life. In 2011 he returned to work, school, and soccer. He has coached for Sonoma Academy and Stony Point Academy. He loves working with the youth of today and watching them grow and develop their soccer skills and take advantage of opportunities in school and life. He is currently engaged and living with his fiancée in Rohnert Park. Coach Salazar still plays soccer, is a massage therapist, and dedicates his time coaching with Stony Point Academy. He hopes to be with Stony Point Academy for many years to come!
