LET US FIND YOUR PATH FOR SUCCESS

Lori Olson

Counselor

Ms. Olson earned her B.A. in Sociology from the University of Colorado Boulder, and her M.A. in School Counseling from the University of San Diego where she was an intern for two years. She has been working as a professional school counselor for three years in K-8th schools. She is excited to work with 7th-12th grade students, and to help them pursue their dreams after graduation while supporting their needs that arise throughout their years at Stony Point Academy. In her spare time you can find her hiking, traveling, cooking, or hanging out with her friends.

Hello

Hello Stony Point Academy! My name is Lori Olson, and I will be the SPA counselor this year. I have been a counselor for 5 years, and this year I am excited to help students with their college and career plans. I have been wanting to help students achieve their post-graduation dreams for a long time. I look forward to helping students with the stuff that is going on in their lives. For the last two years I have been the counselor at Meadow View Elementary school, and I have loved getting to know this community. This year I will also be supervising the counselors at Kawana, Meadow View, Taylor Mountain, and Bellevue. Please come by and say hi anytime!
