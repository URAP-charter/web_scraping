Humes Preparatory Academy

At HPA, we prepare our scholars to be college-ready, career-ready, and community-ready because big dreams deserve dedicated support.

Learn More

Humanitarianism • Technology • Science • Fine Arts

Our Story

Since 2012, when Humes Middle School converted to Gordon Science & Arts Academy, and then to Humes Preparatory Academy in partnership with the Achievement School District, we’ve been empowering scholars with academic skills, personal values, and self-confidence to succeed in our ever-changing world.
