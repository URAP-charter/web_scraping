The Top-Rated K–12 Virtual School in Nevada

Students throughout Nevada are thriving at Nevada Connections Academy (NCA), a tuition-free virtual public school that gives students the flexibility to learn with a curriculum that meets rigorous state education standards.

Nevada Connections Academy offers:

  • A challenging K-12 curriculum developed by leading education experts.
  • Instruction from state-certified teachers experienced in online instruction.
  • Support from trained counselors, principals, and administrative staff.
  • Free textbooks, curriculum materials, and computer equipment needed to participate in a dynamic online learning environment.

Welcome to Nevada Connections Academy

NCA is not just an exceptional Nevada virtual school. It’s a tight knit school community offering all the services and resources that create a well-rounded student experience:

  • Dedicated highly qualified teachers who, in addition to their teaching duties, travel the state for school field trips, locally-based state test administration, field days, social events, and more.
  • A national Connections Academy peer-to-peer tutoring program where students can receive one-on-one assistance in a LiveLesson® virtual classroom.
  • Extensive writing workshops to help students in grades 5, 8, and 11 master the skills needed for academic success and state testing requirements.
  • Weekly “Lunch Bunch” gatherings throughout the state where students, families, and teachers connect for games, lunch, and conversation.
  • Dozens of clubs and activities that foster social development and academic growth.
  • An involved community of students and their families.

Our Mission

The mission of Nevada Connections Academy is to help each student maximize his or her potential and meet the highest performance standards through a uniquely individualized learning program at our virtual school in Nevada.

School Facts

Nevada Connections Academy
(NCA) is a free public online school serving students in grades K–12 throughout Nevada.

  • Teaching center and administrative office in Reno.
  • The top-rated K–12 Virtual School in Nevada.
  • Over 93% of NCA parents agree that the Connections Academy curriculum is high quality. View our parent survey results.
