Secretary-Margaret Quist

Mother of 6, Grandmother of 22. Resident of the Wolf Creek/Sunny Valley area for 8 years. Former Innkeeper of the Wolf Creek Inn. Volunteered many hours starting up the school. Former Board member of the OPC and current board member of SWCS, serving as secretary. Dedicated to support the students, staff and director of SWCS and to provide the highest quality of education to the marvelous children in our community. I am honored to be a board member.
