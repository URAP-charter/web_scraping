Visit Ferris State University

The Ferris Daily Visit Program includes:

  • Admissions Presentation
  • Campus Tour
  • Lunch at The Rock Cafe

The Ferris Midafternoon Visit Program includes:

  • Admissions Presentation
  • Campus Tour

Major Information Sessions:

Ferris is now offering the opportunity to meet with popular academic areas as part of your visit.  The sessions are available as shown below.  Please sign up when you choose your Ferris Daily Visit.

Monday- Dental Hygiene, Mechanical Engineering Tech., Welding Engineering Tech.
Tuesday- Career Exploration & General Studies, College of Arts & Sciences, Plastics & Rubber Engineering Tech, CAD Drafting & Tool Design
Thursday- Architecture & Facility Management, Construction Dept., Criminal Justice, Graphic Design, Management Dept., Marketing Dept.

To Sign Up:

1) Click "search". Use the "+" symbol to add guests to your event.
2) If applicable, use the "+" symbol to attend a Major Information Session. Click "Continue".
3) Complete the initial form.
4) Click "next" and complete event confirmation form. 
5) You will be sent a confirmation email following your submission.

Please notify the Admissions Office at 231-591-3400 at least 24 hours before your scheduled visit if you require special accommodations. The walking tour is one hour and there are stairs throughout the tour.
