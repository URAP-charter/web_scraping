Getting Help - By Text Message

A real-time "Text a Librarian" reference service is available for the Ferris State University community during the following hours while classes are in session. This is also known as short message service (SMS).

  • Sunday: 1 p.m. - 9 p.m.
  • Monday - Thursday: 10 a.m. - 9 p.m.
  • Friday: 10 a.m. - 5 p.m.
  • Saturday: 12 p.m. - 5 p.m.

During semester breaks the hours are Monday - Friday: 10 a.m. - 5 p.m.

Our phone number for this service is: (231) 821-5499
