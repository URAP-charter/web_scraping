Did You Know?

Books which can be checked out from the library can be found on the lower level and on the third floor. Call numbers A-D are on the first floor, and books with call numbers E-Z are mostly on the third floor. Some smaller collections are in the lower level.


Scanning Stations

For those patrons who need to scan information, near the Oval Information Desk on the first floor are two scanning stations with appropriate software. These stations are available on a first come, first serve basis. There is no charge associated with using these computer workstations and you do not need to show a Bulldog ID card.

For assistance with technical issues, look to staff seated at the Oval Information Desk.
