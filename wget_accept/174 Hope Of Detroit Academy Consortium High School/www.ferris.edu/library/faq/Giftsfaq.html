Donations

The FSU Library for Information Technology and Education (FLITE) is grateful for your consideration to donate materials to our collection. Items that meet the needs of our users are always welcome.

It is important to know that the items donated may not fit the needs of the library and, therefore may be disposed of at the discretion of the library. At the time of donation, you must read and complete the donation form, which transfers the ownership of donations to the library. Items we do not add to our collection include newspapers, old textbooks, popular fiction, random issues of journals (except to fill in missing issues), duplicates of materials we already own and damaged items.
