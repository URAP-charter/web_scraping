Lost Materials

Lost books or materials should be reported promptly.

Cost of Lost Items:

Actual replacement cost if known or average replacement cost (according to Bowkers Annual) will be charged for all lost materials. There will also be a $20.00 processing fee charged and a $20.00 service fee charged to the patron.

Refunds:

If a book is returned six months or later, no refunds are made. The cost of the book and the processing fee are refunded if you return it within six months. Service fees will not be refunded.
