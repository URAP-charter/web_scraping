Study Rooms

Study rooms are available throughout the facility. Some rooms are equipped with TV/VCRs/DVD players. The rooms are available for faculty, staff, and students on a first come, first serve basis, for four hours. The late fee for the study rooms is $15 per hour.

You will need to check out a key at the Check Out desk to access the study room. Keys need to be returned at the end of the blocked time; otherwise late fees will be charged.
