Oval Information Desk

If you need assistance using a computer, FLITE computer technicians can help you with a variety of issues. If you need research help, librarians are also available. They can help you locate and use both traditional and electronic information resources. You may also contact a reference librarian for help by calling (231) 591-3602, by sending an email, or by talking live with a librarian through our chat reference service. For more information on the research help available in FLITE, please see Reference Services button below.
