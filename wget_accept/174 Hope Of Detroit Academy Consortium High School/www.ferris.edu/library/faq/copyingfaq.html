Copy Machines

Copy machines are available in all public areas of FLITE. Some are coin operated and others use an account.

Copy Costs

  • 10¢ per page 8½ x 11 or 8½ x 14 photocopies
  • 20¢ per page for 11 x 17 black & white photocopies
  • 25¢ per page for 8½ x 11 color photocopies
  • 50¢ per page for 11 x 17 color photocopies
  • 15¢ per page for microfilm/microfiche prints
  • 10¢ per page for black & white computer prints
  • 25¢ per page for color computer prints
  • 50¢ per page for faxing (local, long distance, international)

Printing/Copy Accounts

  • Ferris students, faculty, and staff can use their Ferris computing username and password to login to the copier.
  • Patrons not affiliated with Ferris State University can create a printing account that can be used to operate the copy machine. Ask at a 1st floor service desk for assistance in setting up an account.
  • There is no fee to create the account
  • Coin operated copiers are also located on the 2nd and 3rd floors, just inside and to the left from the double doors from the elevator corridor. These can be used in lieu of a printing account.
  • No cash refunds. Only deposit the amount of cash you expect to use during your time at Ferris State University. The minimum amount is one dollar.
