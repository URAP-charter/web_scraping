Accessibility

FLITE is ADA accessible.
If you need assistance, please ask a staff member. Parking for users with disabilities is available near the entrance with the glass pyramid dome. Elevators are located near the main lobby. Wheelchair accessible restrooms are located next to the elevators on all floors. A portable wheelchair is available at the Check Out Desk.

The Adaptive Technologies Lab has been developed in conjunction with the Disabilities Services Office specifically to aid students and other library users with disabilities.
