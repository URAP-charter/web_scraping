ILL- Interlibrary Loans

Ferris State University students, faculty, staff, and emeriti can request library materials not owned by Ferris via the Interlibrary Loan (ILL) office. All requests are entered and managed through an online system known as ILLiad.

Photocopy requests must comply with Title 17 U.S. Code Guidelines and other provisions of the Copyright Law. Allow approximately one week for delivery after placing ILL request forms.
