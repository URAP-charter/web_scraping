FSU Media Self-Booking

The DVDs and VHS tapes formerly housed at Media Distribution are currently held in secure (e.g., non regular-circulating collection) areas of FLITE. These items have been fully cataloged and can be self-booked for classroom use on our Online Catalog Booking Module. Instructions on how to use the Booking Module are provided in a link below. There is also a link to go directly to the portion of the catalog containing the records of the media.

You can pick up your items from the check-out desk in FLITE beginning approximately 24 hours before your class.
