Borrowing a Laptop Computer

FLITE has laptop computers available for checkout. One can be borrowed from the Check Out desk for a period of four hours. These laptops will connect to the wireless network and have the capability to print using the PaperCut system within the building. The laptops are for In-FLITE use only, not in the Extended Hours Study Area.

Ferris State University faculty, staff, and students are eligible to borrow a FLITE laptop. A valid Ferris ID will be required, and you will be required to agree to and sign a usage agreement. A copy of the agreement can be obtained from the Check Out desk.
