Distance Education

Distance Education Library Services provides library support to Ferris students, faculty and staff at extension sites. We also provide library support for clinical work off-site and Internet-based classes. There are many resources available to assist you in finding, retrieving and evaluating information and much of it is available from the Web!

To access Library resources for distance education, including our article databases, please make sure that you have a valid MyFSU ID and password. You may also use a Ferris ID card with the library barcode and pin number. If you have any difficulties, please contact the distance education librarian at (231) 591-3635.
