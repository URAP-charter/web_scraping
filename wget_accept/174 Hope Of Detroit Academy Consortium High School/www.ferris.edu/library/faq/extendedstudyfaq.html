Extended Study Area

The room off the main entrance to FLITE with the mural is open for extended hours to registered Ferris students with valid ID. Computer workstations are available. During Fall and Spring Semesters, (after the first month of classes), the Extended Study Area is usually open Monday – Thursday from midnight to 7:00 am. Weekend and summer hours may vary.

Family Court: Patrons with young children may wish to study in this child-friendly space, which is located off the Extended Study Area. Please do not leave children unattended.
