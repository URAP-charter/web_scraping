Parking for FLITE

Lot 27, located immediately southwest of the front of the Ferris Library for Information, Technology and Education, is the main parking area for FLITE. This lot is a commuter lot which contains a mixture of student parking and faculty/staff parking with appropriate permits. There is only metered parking available for visitors.

Additional FLITE visitor and faculty/staff parking can be found in Lot 10 (located next to Williams Auditorium off Cedar Street), and Lot 31 & 32, Metered Parking, (located next to Rankin Center). All lots require passes which may be picked up anytime at the Public Safety Office, 800 Ferris Drive. They are open 24-hours a day.

Please call Public Safety at (231) 591-5000 for more information.
