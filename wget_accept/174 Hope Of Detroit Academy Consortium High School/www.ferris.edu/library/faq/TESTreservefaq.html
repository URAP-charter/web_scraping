Course Reserve Materials

FLITE Library provides a course reserve service to support teaching and learning activities at Ferris State University. Course materials can be place on either hard copy or electronic reserve. To locate a faculty members reserves use the reserves tab in the online catalog and search by either course # or the faculty member's last name. Hard copy reserve materials may be borrowed from the first floor checkout desk. These circulate for two hours within the library, unless otherwise designated. Students may borrow only two course review items at one time.
