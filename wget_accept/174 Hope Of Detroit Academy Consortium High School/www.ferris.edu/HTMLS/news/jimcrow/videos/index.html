From Symbols of Hate to Portraits of Understanding

Dr. David Pilgrim's 6-part speech at a forum in Detroit hosted by the NAACP.

April 30, 2011 — The Freedom Institue presented a national forum entitled "From Symbols of Hate to Portraits of Understanding." This national discussion began with a special presentation from Dr. David Pilgrim of Ferris State University entitled: "Them: Images of Separation", sponsored by the Jim Crow Museum of Racist Memorabilia.
