Event Planning

Planning an event at Ferris State University is an investment into the relationship building between numerous individuals and the university. A campus event can help steward donors, reconnect alumni, celebrate accomplishments, unveil new buildings or programs and build long lasting relationships with current students. The following guide will provide suggested steps to take before, during and after an event to ensure the event is successful and appears as seamless as possible.
