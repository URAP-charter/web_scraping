Business Policies

Welcome to the Administration and Finance Business Policy Website

The University Business Policies apply to the actions of the University community but are developed and administered under the direction of the Administration and Finance Division. For questions related to a particular policy, please look for the contact information at the end of each document.

If you would like to suggest a new policy, or prepare revisions to an existing policy, please contact Lisa Brady at LisaBrady@ferris.edu or 231-591-2164.
