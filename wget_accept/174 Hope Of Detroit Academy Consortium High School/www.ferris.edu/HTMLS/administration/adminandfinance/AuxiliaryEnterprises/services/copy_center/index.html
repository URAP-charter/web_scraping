Copy Center


The FSU Copy & Print Center provides  copy service for classroom materials, memos, newsletters, reports, flyers and brochures. Other services include color copies, folding, collating, stapling, punching, perforating, inserting envelopes for mailing, business reply envelopes and cards, tabbing unibinding and transparencies. The Copy Center can print on envelopes, up to 13 x 19 inch paper, and carbonless paper (2-part to 5-part). We can print yard signs, banners, large posters, and we carry more than 140 different colors of paper in various weights and textures.

In addition to its walk-up counter, the FSU Copy & Print Center delivers finished work and picks up new work at 16 locations on campus twice daily. Special delivery of large projects or direct delivery to the Mailroom also is available. Our hours of operation are 7:30am to 5:30pm, Monday through Friday. You can email your work to the Copy Center via email and have it delivered to your building (or you can pick it up if you prefer).

We are now accepting work orders online. The online work order form is as easy to use as our regular paper form. This will make it easier to place orders from the comfort of your office or home. With our new system, you can place an order, edit it or check its status online.

FSU Copy & Print Center offers walk-in service for faculty, staff, and students for "while you wait" copies. If you have materials that need to be done right away, you can be assured that we will do everything possible to meet your deadlines.

*All jobs are printed in-house.
