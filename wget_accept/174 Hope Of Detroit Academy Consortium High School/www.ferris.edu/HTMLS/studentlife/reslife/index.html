Housing Contracting 2017-18

Early Contracting has passed but there’s still time to sign up and stay on campus next year. Living on campus allows students to fully immerse in the university experience from study sessions in the halls to special events and activities around campus.

Hey Juniors and Seniors*, receive a FREE PARKING PERMIT for the 2017-18 school year when you sign your housing contract and choose an Anytime Meal Plan or 125 Express Meal Plan before the end of the academic year. Follow these steps for the FREE permit.

Summer Housing

Summer contracting is now open. If you are working on campus or taking classes over the summer, you could qualify for free housing. See if you qualify for free housing and view rates for East Campus Suites and newly reduced rates West Campus Apartments.

North Hall

We are proud to announce the construction project of a 402 bed Residence Hall on the North end of campus. North Hall is scheduled to be completed by the end of Summer 2017 and ready for new Freshman to move in Fall 2017. The goal of the project is to provide residents with quality housing and easy access to many frequented destinations. Visit fsunorthhall.com to learn about the project, schedule, logistics, and view progress photos or watch live on the webcam.
