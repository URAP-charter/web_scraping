FSUS Instructors

Ferris State University Seminar - the Foundation for College Success!

FSUS is a class designed to help students make the transition from high school and home to the university community. This seminar course addresses the following topics:

  • Campus and community connections
  • Networking with peers and professionals
  • Improving appreciation for diverse cultures and ethnic groups
  • Wellness and personal responsibility
  • Using the FLITE library
  • Individual learning preferences and college study methods
  • Class registration
  • Academic integrity
  • The core values of Ferris State University
  • Student engagement
  • Financial Awareness
