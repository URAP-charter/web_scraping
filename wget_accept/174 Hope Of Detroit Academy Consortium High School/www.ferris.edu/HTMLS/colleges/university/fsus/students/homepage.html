Ferris State University Seminar - the Foundation for College Success!

Active participation in FSUS assists students in:

Facilitating
Facilitating a smooth transition into college life
Supporting
Supporting personal wellness and responsibility
Utilizing
Utilizing academic and personal support services
Strategizing
Strategizing for academic success

4 out of 5 students report that FSUS has contributed to improving their understanding of their own academic strengths and the impact of establishing personal goals. In addition, FSUS has increased their understanding of campus policies, procedures, and resources as well as contributed to their ability to adjust to the college social environment. (2007-2008 First-Year Initiative Survey)

You, too, will benefit by actively participating in an FSUS course! The links at the left of this page will help you to succeed in your educational journey at Ferris State University.
