Class Notes

Share your news and photos with fellow alumni!

Have you recently been promoted? Married? Or had a child? If so then share your information with us and we may feature your advancement in the next issue of Ferris Magazine. Please send the following: news, name (maiden name), phone, email, address and college/year graduated to alumnioffice@ferris.edu

Update Your Information

Have you moved? Received a new telephone number or job title? Or just want to make sure we have your most current information? Email us at alumnioffice@ferris.edu with your current information. We are always updating our files and looking for the most current address, business address, telephone, and email. Please remember to list your maiden name (if applicable) and your graduate year and degree.

Distinguished Alumni Award Nomination Form

The Ferris State University Alumni Association annually recognizes outstanding alumni of the University based upon the following criteria:

  • Professional Growth
  • Leadership
  • Contributions to Community, Society and Ferris State University

The Purpose of the awards is to:

  • Recognize alumni who have used their Ferris degree, whether in their vocation or avocation, to better themselves, society or the University
  • Provide role models for our current students
  • Cultivate those individuals who have benefited from their FSU education to give back to the University so that current students will have those same advantages

Please complete the online Nomination Form to recognize an outstanding alumnus/alumna for a Distinguished Alumni Award.

Pacesetter Award Nomination Form

The Pacesetter Award recognizes Distinguished Alumni who are recent graduates of the University (12 years or less). The same criteria for the Distinguished Alumni Awards is used for the Pacesetter Awards. These awards were established by the Alumni Association to recognize those recent graduates who are "setting the pace" in their communities, their professions and our society as a whole.

 Find A Classmate

Searching for a fellow classmate or old professor? Let us help you assist in your search. Please provide us with as much information as possible and we will attempt to contact the person on your behalf and provide them with your contact information so that they may contact you in return if they wish.

Please email the alumni office at alumnioffice@ferris.edu with the following information:

Contact Information: First name, last name, address and telephone number. Please specify how you would like to be contacted (email, telephone, mail) and your graduation year from FSU.

Lost Person Information: First name, last name or maiden name, and approximate graduation year and program if known. Please include a message to the lost person that we can include in our outreach efforts.

Legacy Scholarship Application

The Alumni Association Legacy Scholarship recognizes academic achievements and leadership potential in students whose parent, sibling or grandparent is a Ferris State University graduate.

Scholarship Amount and Duration:

The scholarship is a one-time award that can be up to $2,000 and will be presented in two equal increments for two consecutive semesters. The student must be enrolled full-time and be making satisfactory degree progress.

Conditions of Eligibility:

To be considered for this competitive scholarship a student must:

  1. Be the child, sibling or grandchild of a Ferris Alumna(us).
  2. Have a 2.75 cumulative Grade Point Average.
  3. Have successfully completed a minimum of two full-time semesters. Please note:  
    • Students seeking an associate degree must have completed 30 hours at the end of spring semester.
    • Students seeking a bachelor's degree must have completed 56 hours at the end of spring semester.
    • Preference will be give to students in their final year at FSU.
  4. Submit this application including an essay describing personal career goals and the role a Ferris State degree will play in the achievement of these goals.
  5. Submit a letter from the Ferris State University alumna(us) parent describing the effect their educational experiences at Ferris had on their life or professional career.
  6. Submit the attached recommendation by a Ferris Faculty or Staff person.
  7. Have not received this scholarship award previously.

Important dates for the 2015-2016 academic year:

Application posted online:  Mid to late January

May 1, 2015 – Application deadline.

July 2015 – Scholarship recipients are selected and announced.

September 2015 - Legacy Luncheon honoring recipients.
