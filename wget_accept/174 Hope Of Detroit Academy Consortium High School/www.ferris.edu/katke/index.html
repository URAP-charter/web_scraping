Katke Golf Course

A Nationally Renowned University Golf Course

With 21 holes of championship golf and an award-winning practice range, Katke Golf Course at Ferris State University is one of the finest golf learning facilities in the Midwest. Beginning to expert golfers will find the right amount of challenge on our greens. Our golf course will improve your game and take you to the next level of play. We'll meet your challenge while helping you out. Learn more about our course.

Visit us at Katkegolf.com
