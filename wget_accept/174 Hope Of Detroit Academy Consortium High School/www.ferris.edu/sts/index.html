Welcome Students!


Your use and access to technology is a vital part of your life at Ferris. We endeavor to help you achieve your academic goals by providing a growing number of technological services and resources to help you with your schoolwork and make your use of technology as painless as possible.

Please explore our site to learn about the services we provide, like repair service, free and low-cost software, discounts on computers, wireless access, and more...
