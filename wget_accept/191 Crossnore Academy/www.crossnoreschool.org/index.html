Here, miracles happen every day!

For more than 100 years Crossnore School & Children’s Home has provided a Christian sanctuary of hope and healing for children in need from North Carolina. Our holistic model of care allows us to care for and nurture each child’s mind, body and spirit. Please visit us the next time you are in the High Country or in Winston-Salem, NC – you’ll quickly realize that we are so much more than a children’s home!
