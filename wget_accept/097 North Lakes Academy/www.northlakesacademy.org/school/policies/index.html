Policies

Home > Board > Policies

North Lakes Academy's Policy Committee is charged by our School Board to review, update, and standardize all of our school's policies. Some documents may contain highlighted portions, which signify ongoing edits being considered by the School Board. These portions do not represent approved changes, but could be undergoing first or second reads by the Board. 

If you have questions, please contact Christopher Stewart, our Secretary and Policy Committee member.
