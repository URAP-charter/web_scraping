Home > Students > Student Clubs > MS Art Club

Art Club meets every other Monday after school from 2:30 - 3:30. A schedule is listed below. Each meeting will focus of a different activity so there is no need to attend every Art Club meeting in order to participate. 

Participants will be working with many fun and different (possibly messy) materials such as spray paint, wax, hot glue, nail polish, duct-tape, tie-dye and tons more!

There will be a snack provided, but a fee of $15 is suggested in order to provide supplies and to cover the cost of these snacks.

Contact Lisa Gatzke and/or Robin Jones for more details!
