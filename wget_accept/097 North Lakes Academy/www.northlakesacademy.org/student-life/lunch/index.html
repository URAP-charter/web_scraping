We are happy to be offering the Federal free and reduced lunch program. Lunch is provided by Lancer Dining Services. They specialize in balanced healthy meals that are delivered hot and ready to serve that include fresh fruits and vegetables that kids love to eat. 

Full price student lunches cost $3.50 and free-and-reduced lunches are provided at no cost. Log in to PowerSchool to check your balance.

***NLA is now offering breakfast! Breakfast is served daily from 7:25-7:45 a.m. Breakfast is free for those students who qualify for free/reduced lunch.***
