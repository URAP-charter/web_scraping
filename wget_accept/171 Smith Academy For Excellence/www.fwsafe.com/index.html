  Smith Academy for Excellence

Welcome
 
Smith Academy for Excellence (SAFE) is a tuition-free, all boys charter school located in Fort Wayne, IN. The school takes advantage of family and community support to administer a strong comprehensive academic curriculum that includes character education and service learning projects, which will develop young men into lifelong leaders.

The school strategically develops elite scholars of responsibility, dignity, character and service. These young men will be positive leaders of consequence in Fort Wayne’s community.
"Smith Academy for Excellence has been very positive for our son. [He] has excelled both academically and socially. [He] has become very involved in the clubs that are offered as well as the sports. Those who attend Smith Academy for Excellence are not a number, they are a person."
                                                                                                                  -SAFE Parents
