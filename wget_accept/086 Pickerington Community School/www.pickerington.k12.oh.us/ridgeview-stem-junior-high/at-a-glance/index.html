About Ridgeview

Our Mission

Ridgeview is an extraordinary school filled with many opportunities for students. Through our continued STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) focus and quality instructional practices, we work to create a community where students experience a challenging learning environment. All students will have access to authentic learning opportunities that make real world connections through a variety of disciplines and are engaged in innovative thinking and problem solving. This philosophy supports students in becoming college and career ready by the time they graduate high school.

Attendance Reporting

Parents/guardians are asked to notify the school by 9:00 AM when their student is going to be absent.  You may contact our 24-hour Attendance Hotline at 614-548-1701.

Daily Class Schedule

Our school day begins at  7:20 AM and ends at 2:15 PM.  Our bell schedule consists of seven class periods including lunch.

On days with a two-hour delay, classes start at 9:20 a.m. and dismissal is at 2:15 p.m.
