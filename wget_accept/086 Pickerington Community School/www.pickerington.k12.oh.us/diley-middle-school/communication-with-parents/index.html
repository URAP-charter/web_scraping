Celebration of Excellence/ State of the Schools

The evening will begin at 6:30 p.m. with the third annual Celebration of Excellence Expo and community networking opportunity in PAC lobby. Every school in the district will exhibit innovative and exciting programs from their buildings. Displays will range from student leadership programs to arts initiatives, from science and technology to after-school programs.

At 7:15 p.m., Superintendent Valerie Browning-Thompson, Treasurer Ryan Jenkins, and Business Manager Vince Utterback will present the 2017 State of the Schools, a 60-minute presentation covering subjects ranging from the district’s financial outlook to academic initiatives and plans for the future. The presentation also will include an overview of the 3-mill permanent improvements levy that has been placed on the May 2 ballot.

A question-and-answer session will follow the address.

The presentation will open and close with musical performances by the Pickerington High School Central Choir and Pickerington High School North Jazz Band.

Whose up for a challenge?

Diley Middle School would like to start a WordMasters Team. The WordMasters Challenge™ is a vocabulary competition based on completing analogies. The goals of this team are to:

  • grow your vocabulary
  • learn how words are related
  • challenge yourself by developing verbal reasoning skills through the use of analogies
  • have fun!

 

The team would meet weekly after school on Wednesdays from 3:30 to 4:15 starting January 11. At these meetings students will be involved in hands-on, challenging activities designed to help them learn the vocabulary. Eventually students will take part in three WordMasters Challenges where they put into practice what they have learned.
If you are interested,  please fill out the permission form that was sent home with students.  The deadline is January 6th.

Bus Information

Petermann Transportation has updated its mobile app, which parents can use to track their children’s buses. The new version can be downloaded at  Bus Tracker

Parents will need their children’s student ID numbers to set up the app.

The new app improves functionality from the old version, including giving Petermann Transportation the ability to communicate directly with parents about bus delays and other issues.

Parents are reminded that they should not use the system to wait until the very last minute to send their children to the bus stop. Students must be at their designated stop by the scheduled bus arrival time, and waiting until the last minute can be dangerous if children are running to meet their bus.

Plus, the system operates by tracking the GPS units in each bus, so it operates on a lag that can be up to two minutes. During that time, the bus may have made progress on its route, so GPS locations are only approximate.

Hello Parents,

My name is Aileen Targett, and I am the Theatre Teacher at Pickerington Central. This year we have chosen “A Christmas Carol”, for the Fall musical. In this musical, there is a need for children younger than high-school age. These roles include Tiny Tim, the Cratchet children, and Town children. All roles will be required to sing, act, and perform simple choreography. Tiny Tim will be double-cast and can be either a girl or a boy.

The auditions will take place on Friday, September 9th at 4:00 P.M. Please report to the PAC for the auditions. Guardians will be required to fill out paper work upon arrival. Your child will learn a small section of music and will be provided a short excerpt from the play to read. Some children will be asked to stay later to read with members of the cast. Your child will be called into a connecting room to audition. We ask that parents and guardians remain in the theatre during their child’s audition.

If your child is selected to be in the musical you will be provided with a schedule. Performance dates are November 30th through December 4th. Please note that there is a matinee on the 2nd. Your child must be there for the matinee. Please also note, two weeks leading up to the first performance, I will need your child to be present at all rehearsals. Through September and October your child will be needed two times a week. Though,Tiny Tim will be needed more frequently.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to e-mail me. On Wednesday, August 31st, I will be in my room at Pickerington Central from 4:00 – 5:00 if you would like to meet me in person. I do ask that if you intend on coming that you please e-mail me by Monday the 29th.

Thank you so much for your time, and I hope to see you and your little ones at auditions.

Sincerely,
Aileen Targett
