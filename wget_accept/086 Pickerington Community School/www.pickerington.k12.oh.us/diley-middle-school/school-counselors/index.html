Diley Middle School Counselor's Page

Dear DMS Families,

This fall I will be moving on to take a position in a different district. It has truly been a pleasure to serve the students of Diley Middle School. Though the move is bittersweet, I am pleased to announce that the administration team was able to find a great counselor to take over the position. Gina Mascari will be taking over the position as school counselor effective August 2016. Ms. Mascari is an amazing educator and will be great for the students and families at Diley Middle School. Again, I would like to thank you for the opportunity to serve as counselor at Diley. I wish you all the best in the future.


Candun Griffin

Before & After School Group Opportunities

Student Council

Student Council – Student Council is a service leadership group for both 5th and 6th grade students. One student is chosen from each home room to serve as student council representative for the year. This group meets twice a month afterschool to discuss and plan various service projects throughout the year as well as learn about different characteristics leadership and success. Students must apply for the opportunity to participate in this group.

Schedule 

Ladies First

Ladies First is a group that gives the young ladies of Diley an opportunity to meet with myself and more importantly, other women from our building and the community. This year our ladies will meet to discuss the 7 habits of highly effective teens. This group promotes the discussion and practice of etiquette and good character. The group meets twice a month before school. The first meeting of the month will be held in the Library. For this meeting, students are asked to dress up. The second meeting consists of playing a game or doing some other physical activity in the gym, as well as a recap of the topics discussed at the beginning of the month. Students are encouraged to attend both meetings to emphasize the importance of values and skills being learned in the first meeting, as well as reap the benefit of physical activity and camaraderie amongst peers in the second meeting.

Young Gents

Before School

Young Gents is a group that gives the young men of Diley an opportunity to meet with myself and other men from our building and the community. This year our young men will meet to discuss the 7 habits of highly effective teens. This group promotes the discussion and practice of etiquette and good character. The group meets twice a month before school. The first meeting of the month will be held in the Library. For this meeting, students are asked to dress up in a shirt and tie. The second meeting consists of playing a game or doing some other physical activity in the gym, as well as a recap of the topics discussed at the beginning of the month. Students are asked to attend both meetings to emphasize the importance of values and skills being learned in the first meeting, as well as reap the benefit of physical activity and camaraderie amongst peers in the second meeting.

Lunch Group Opportunities

Groups

All small groups will be held during student lunch/recess periods on specified days below.

5th Grade students will meet on Tuesdays

6th Grade students will meet on Thursdays

New Students/Building Friendships (September) – This group will discuss the following topics: what’s unique about Diley Middle School/PLSD, explore different ways to get involved at DMS, conversation starters, and different aspects of being a good friend.

5th Grade – 9/1, 9/8, 9/15, 9/22

6th Grade – 9/3, 9/10, 9/17, 9/24

 

Academic Success (October) – This group will discuss the following topics: Organization, time management, test taking strategies, and study habits.

5th Grade – 10/6, 10/13, 10/20, 10/27

6th Grade – 10/8, 10/15, 10/22, 10/29

 

Empower – self esteem (November/December) – This group will discuss the following topics: strengths & weaknesses, the power of positive thinking, and how to put yourself in a position to be successful.

5th Grade – 11/10, 11/17, 12/1, 12/8

6th Grade – 11/12, 11/19, 12/3, 12/10

 

Families in Transition (January) – This group will discuss the following topics: how to manage differences between households, not playing the middle man, understanding that you are not to blame, and not being defined by circumstance

5th Grade – 1/5, 1/12, 1/19, 1/26

6th Grade – 1/7, 1/14, 1/21, 28

 

Stand up Against Bullying (February) – This group will discuss the following topics: T different types of bullying, how not to be a bystander, and how to handle different bulling situations.

5th Grade – 2/2, 2/9, 2/16, 2/23

6th Grade – 2/4, 2/2/11, 2/18, 2/2/25

 

Career Exploration (March) – This group will discuss the following topics: strengths and interests, personality type, exploring different career clusters based on your personality type and interests.

5th Grade – 3/1, 3/ 8, 3/15

6th Grade – 3/3, 3/15, 3/17

 

Transitions – {6th grade only} (April) – This group will discuss the following topics: navigating the Jr. High, organization and study skills, and student schedules.

6th Grade – 4/7, 4/14, 4/21, 4/28
