Diley Middle School PTSO

Diley Middle School PTSO has some exciting things planned for the upcoming school year. We encourage everyone to join the PTSO. Membership is only $5 and goes a long way in enabling us to provide fun programs and activities for the students at DMS. Being a member also gives you the ability to vote and provide input on what you would like to see at Diley. During the school year we strive to provide opportunities for parents and grandparents to be involved as much or as little as they want to be. If you are interested in getting more involved, please download our membership form and our volunteer applications. Or you can e-mail the PTSO president for more information. Thank you for your interest in Diley PTSO.

AR Store

Chairperson and their committee shops for prizes kids can use AR Points to purchase them with; schedules volunteers and works the event each nine weeks; they also complete an inventory at the end of the school year.

Volunteer – Sign up to work at the store during designated timeslots; the store is held at the end of each 9 weeks.

Book Fair

Help students shop for books and collect money from purchases – one in fall and one in spring.

Fall Fundraiser

Chairperson will work with rep to put on sale this includes making flyers, having a launch day and distributing information; also includes keeping track of orders/sales by class and awarding prizes; any extra promotion is optional; Chairperson also is responsible for collecting on bounced checks etc.

Volunteers — Help chairperson distribute fundraiser and prizes on designated days in the fall.

Family Fun Night

Chairperson determines activities and plans the event.

Volunteer – Run a station for the kids at this one-day event in November (stations are assigned by the committee chair).

Parent Luncheons

Volunteer to help set up for the event and to serve drinks as needed (two times in the fall and two times in the spring).

Spelling Bee

Volunteer to serve refreshments – also opportunity to donate cookies.

Spirit Wear Sale

Chairperson works with rep to determine what will be sold, takes orders, sort orders and distribute orders.

Spring Fun Night

Chairpersons determine activities and plan the event.

Volunteer – Run a station for the kids at this one-day event in the Spring (stations are assigned by the committee chair).

Staff Appreciation

Chairperson develops ideas, determine activities and plan the events for staff; develop a committee to assist with projects.

Volunteer – Donate food when needed, help assemble gifts and help with set up (you determine if you are available).

Super Games

Chairperson works with the principal and the president to select a date for this one day event held in the fall. Chairperson also coordinates volunteers, develops a sign up, and works with the company to organize the event.

2016 date will be 9/30/16

Talent Show

Help during show, keeping kids in order and getting acts in order and music assembled.

VIP

Assist the office and teachers with copying and other small projects. (You work with the teacher to determine which days and times you want to volunteer.)

Yearbook

Chairperson helps organize student volunteers, help students design book, help students coordinate and collect photos, oversee distribution and student ad sales, proofread.

Volunteers – help chairperson as needed.
