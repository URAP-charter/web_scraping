Tours and Open Houses (for applying for the 2017-18 school year)

School Tours

Tours are given from 8:45 - 10:15 AM on Tuesdays beginning in December for parents/guardians of children who are eligible for admission to a K-8 class in the subsequent year (Kindergarteners must turn five on or before September 1). Because space is limited, reservations are required and are limited to adults only.

During a tour, visitors receive an information packet, are provided with an overview of the school and have an opportunity to visit classes in session. An administrator and/or current parent meets with the group to answer questions about the school, curriculum, and admissions procedures.

Tap here to request a tour. Choose three date preferences (below). You will receive an email within one week confirming your actual tour date.
