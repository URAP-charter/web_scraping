Fundraising Events

Fundraising at NOCCS is crucial to bridging the "gap" between the Mission-driven and student-centered program we offer (and budget for), and the funds that the State of California provides for basic educational expenses. We are tuition free, but donor dependent. Fundraising events are collaboratively organized by parents and staff to support critical elements of our education model, including:

  • Low adult-child ratio with Assistant Teachers for each Elementary Grade Level Team
  • Physical Education Instruction
  • Fine and Performing Arts-Integrated Curriculum
  • Outdoor Education and Peacemaking

Annual fundraising plans are developed yearly and typically include five major events / initiatives:

  1. Walk-a-Thon
  2. Annual Fund Drive
  3. Spring Event / Auction
  4. Outdoor Education Fund
  5. New Fundraising Ventures (an incubator for new ideas and trial projects)

Of these initiatives, the Annual Fund Drive operates year-round—with a push in the fall and winter—to raise essential funds through direct cash gifts. Recognizing the economic diversity of our community, participation goals are always emphasized in addition to financial targets, and can be leveraged to secure additional funds. You can make a one-time contribution, register for monthly automatic payments, or pledge to make a contribution at a future time. Click here to donate, or stop by the school office to pick up a paper form. Thank you for your support!
