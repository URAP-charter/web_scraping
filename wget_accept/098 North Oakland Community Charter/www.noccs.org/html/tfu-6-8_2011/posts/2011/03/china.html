China

March 17 2011, 4:47 PM  by Gtremblay

The images and lesson plan below correspond to the second Arts-integrated lesson on China. The lesson focuses on Chinese aesthetics, using the example of the bamboo pen to talk about connections between aesthetics and beliefs. This unit explores many manifestations of culture; from art to functional object (often together), inventions, explorations, endeavors, religious beliefs, etc. For this lesson the students listened to Chinese poetry in translation and then combined their "mucking about" (experimentation) with the new medium of pen and ink with their visualization of specifically Chinese imagery.
