Hello there - The following are the understanding goals and associated performances of understanding for Mr. Tempkin's Winter trimester Teaching for Understanding unit on the cultures of China and India (6th grade Social Studies). Attached you will find the full text of the unit including standards, rubrics, calendars, lesson outlines, etc. Photos, sample work, and video to follow soon!

Unit Understanding Goals & Performances of Understanding: 1. How have the contributions and knowledge of ancient China and India influenced our modern world? Associated Performance(s) of Understanding: A. Looking through class books and our text on the civilizations B. Note-taking information and gathering pictures about the civilizations from non-fiction books, the Internet and our text C. Group book with illustrations and information about the contributions and achievements of the civilizations. Group presentation about this culture to the other half of the class. 2. How did gender, religion and class influence the power and opportunities that people had in ancient China and India? Associated Performance(s) of Understanding: see above 3.
