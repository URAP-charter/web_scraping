Arts Integration Lesson Plans

March 24 2011, 12:42 PM  by Gtremblay

They have been left in .doc form to allow for adaptation in other setting and classrooms; please feel free to to use them in your classroom/s! Most, if not all supplemental materials for these lessons came from the Asian Art Museum Education Program (http://www.asianart.org/educatorresources.htm).>
