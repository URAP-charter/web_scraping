pH Lab

June 9 2011, 4:37 PM  by Gtremblay

Today in Ms. Roman's 8th grade class, more mad scientist adventures commenced with the pH lab. Students were provided with eight mystery liquids to test for acidity or alkalinity by using pH strips and comparing them to a guide. The students guessed which substance was which based on their apparent pH and used that information to draw conclusions about the nature/reactivity of the substance. A wonderful example of the scientific method in action.
