Final Understanding Check

June 10 2011, 11:05 AM  by Gtremblay

One of the wonderful things about NOCCS is the fact that students are constantly reflecting on their knowledge. This helps them not only to develop a sense of pride and ownership over their learning, but also reinforces that knowledge so that it is retained over time. In these videos two students in Ms. Roman's 8th grade science class reflect on two understanding goals from this unit:

1) Kalani talks about UG 3 (What is pH and how is it determined?)
2) Ejahye (EJ) talks about UG 1 (What is the difference between and exothermic and endothermic reaction?)
