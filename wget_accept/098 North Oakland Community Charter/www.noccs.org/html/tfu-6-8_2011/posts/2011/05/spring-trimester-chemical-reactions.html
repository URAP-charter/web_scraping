Spring Trimester: Chemical Reactions

May 5 2011, 3:08 PM  by Gtremblay
TfUChemical Rxns.pdf View this file

The Spring Trimester unit is Natalie Roman's 8th grade Science unit on chemical reactions. The unit ties together science content goals and standards with visual art and Project Zero's Studio Habits of Mind and thinking routines. Here are the understanding goals:

1. What is the difference between a chemical change and a physical change? 2. What is the difference between exothermic and endothermic reactions?
3. What is pH and how is it determined?

Science Process U.G.s embedded in performances of understanding throughout the unit (and year): 4. How do scientists make observations? 5. How do scientists write hypotheses? 6. How do scientists design accurate experiments to answer questions?
