Beginning Chemistry

May 13 2011, 2:51 PM  by Gtremblay

This week was a very exciting and tumultuous time in Ms. Roman's 8th grade Science class, as the students began conducting experiments to determine the nature of certain elements and reactions. These students have been hard at work developing their understanding of the scientific method, and this chemistry unit brings together their skills of observation, their content knowledge, and their ability to generate new understandings from the process of reflection. The arts-integration component documented in the photos below based in bookmaking; students created accordion books in which to document (visually and through writing) their observations, hypotheses, big take-away 'nuggets' from the day, and a studio habits of mind (SHoM) reflection.
