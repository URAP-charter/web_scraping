Chemical/Physical Reactions lab

May 27 2011, 11:02 AM  by Gtremblay

In this Chemistry lab, Ms. Roman has done a fabulous job modeling the scientific method, and has designed the process so the students are conducting their experiments in a manner authentic to the discipline. They are testing the combinations of several compounds, including Phenol Red and CaCl2, recording their observations and hypothesizing about whether the reaction represents a chemical or physical change. In the photos and video below, note the high level of interest and engagement, the way the students are using their accordion booklets, and the kinds of questions they are asking. Stellar work everyone!!
