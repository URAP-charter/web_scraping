Final Understanding Check

June 10 2011, 3:25 PM  by Gtremblay

As a Teaching for Understanding unit draws for a close it is time to revisit the original understanding goals. This reflective process helps the students to recognize how much they've learned as well as cementing that knowledge more firmly; it also serves as an authentic assessment for teachers to see how much has been retained, and how to approach that unit the next time. The videos that follow represent the three understanding goals for this unit - a big thanks to my thoughtful and articulate interviewees!

1) How does water move about the earth? (Alejandro)
2) What are components of healthy water and a healthy watershed?(Marilyn)
3) How do I use strong visuals to affect change around the health of water/watersheds? (Langston)
