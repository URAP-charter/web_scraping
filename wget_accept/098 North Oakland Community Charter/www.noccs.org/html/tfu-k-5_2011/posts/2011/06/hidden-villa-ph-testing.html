Hidden Villa + pH testing

June 7 2011, 11:35 AM  by Gtremblay

Two weeks ago the 2/3 grades went on an overnight outdoor ed. trip to Hidden Villa in Los Altos. The students participated in community-building and got some much-needed fresh air, as well as put their scientist skills to use examining the local watershed. Throughout the course of this unit the students have learned to identify the markers of healthy and unhealthy aqueous environments, and spent a great deal of time documenting the inhabitants of the creek, and testing the pH (acidity) of the water. Before going on the trip, the students constructed observation journals in which to record their findings, a wonderful example of interdisciplinary learning as well as an authentic scientific practice. Check out these photos; what a fantastic experience!
