Students visit to Oakland Museum

April 28 2011, 10:07 AM  by Gtremblay

In order to experience the gold rush first-hand, the 4th and 5th grade visited the Oakland Museum on April 7th and took part in real gold-panning, and got to take home the gold they found! These out-in-the-world experiences are so important in making history real for students; you can see them learning concepts and perspectives instead of fleeting factoids.
