Work begins to create a "miniature" blue whale!

May 13 2011, 5:31 PM  by Mlanders

Students outline , cut, and began making the "skin" of a 17 ft blue whale by layering wax (crayons) on top of wax, then crumpling the paper repeatedly to make it "soft."  We are a third of the way through!
