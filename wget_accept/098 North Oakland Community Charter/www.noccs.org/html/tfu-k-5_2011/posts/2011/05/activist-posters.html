Activist posters

May 24 2011, 9:06 PM  by Mlanders

We brainstormed and selected words that tell how we feel about the ocean and how to protect it.  The class came up with these words all on their own.  When they selected their word they drew an image to show the meaning behind the word in hopes to tell others about the state of the ocean and how to help protect ocean life.  Some images are inspiring showing images of the beauty that is all around us while others depict images of frustration and sadness.
Come check them out in the hallway.   I am amazed by their work!
