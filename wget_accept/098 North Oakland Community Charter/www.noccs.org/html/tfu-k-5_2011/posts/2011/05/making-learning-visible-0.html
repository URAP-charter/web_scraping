Making Learning Visible

May 13 2011, 2:42 PM  by Gtremblay

As evidenced by the wonderful documentation of Ms. Landers'/Jones' classes, we love to make our learning visible at NOCCS! As the 2/3 classes continue to explore the topics of water systems, cycles, and the nature of water conservation/pollution effects, the wonderful teaching team posts their work on the hallway outside the classrooms. This helps students to take pride and ownership in their work, as well as reinforcing their learning every time they see it. The photos below are examples of work on this learning wall: enjoy!
