Spring Trimester: 2/3: Waterworks!

May 3 2011, 11:19 AM  by Gtremblay
Water Unit Plan.pdf View this file

This is a fantastic unit written by the NOCCS 2/3 team, Ember Kelley and Katherine Orr. Here are the understanding goals:
1. How does water move around Earth? 2. What are the components of healthy water and a healthy watershed?
3. How do I use strong visuals to affect change around the health of water and watersheds? You will notice that the unit combines process and content goals, where students are scaffolded in their understanding not only of how local watersheds function and how they are affected by humans, but also how to effectively communicate their knowledge.
Check it out!
