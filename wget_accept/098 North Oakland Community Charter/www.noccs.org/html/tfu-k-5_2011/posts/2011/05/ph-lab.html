pH lab

May 27 2011, 11:26 AM  by Gtremblay

In the land of 2/3, the students in Ms. Orr and Ms. Kelley's classes have been hard at work digging into the way our watersheds work, what water ecosystems need to be healthy, and what constitutes good quality water. In the photos below you can see evidence of the students gathering data from their own lives in the form of the water usage in their homes, and the comparative drawings of a healthy and unhealthy watershed. The video was taken during the prep for the pH lab, where students learn how to test the pH of water gathered from various sources, and determine the implications of their findings. Amazing work everyone!!
