Family Involvement

We believe it takes our entire community to educate our children.

Each family is asked to volunteer a minimum of four hours a month of service to the school. Most families give much more than the minimum. In fact, in an average school year, NOCCS families volunteer a total of over 7,000 hours!


NOCCS families provide a wide array of services that draw on their many talents and interests. Examples include:

  • Serving on the NOCCS Board of Trustees or Board committee
  • Serving lunch
  • Teaching visual arts
  • Assisting in the classroom or office
  • Coordinating fundraising projects
  • Proving tech support to the school
  • Developing a school library
  • Helping us "green" our school
  • Gardening
  • And much more!

Our parent volunteer coordinator works to match each family member with a volunteer task that fits their interests, skills, and work schedule. Our families’ contributions enrich our school and help us strengthen connections between school and home.
