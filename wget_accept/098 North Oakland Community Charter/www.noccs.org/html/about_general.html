General Information

North Oakland Community Charter School
1000 42nd Street
Oakland, CA 94608

phone: (510) 655.0540

fax:      (510) 655.1222

 

email:  office@noccs.org

web:    http://www.noccs.org

NOCCS is a K-8 charter school that is open to all school-aged children residing in California, and as a public school is cost-free for students. Like other Oakland public schools, our school grants admissions preference to Oakland families, and is accountable to parents and the Oakland Unified School District.
