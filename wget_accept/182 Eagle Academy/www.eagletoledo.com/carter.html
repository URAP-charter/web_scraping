Mr. Carter has worked at Eagle Academy as a paraprofessional since the fall of 2013. He is from Detroit, Michigan and he is currently studying Secondary Education with a specializion in English at Bowling Green State University. Here at Eagle Academy, Mr. Carter serves at the Director of the YES Mentoring Program and the Coordinator of the Drama Club! An interesting fact about Mr. Carter is that he founded a choir at BGSU, The Voices at BGSU! In his free time, he enjoys singing, watching movies, and eating fried chicken. You can find Mr. Carter in the high school in various classes or in the Support Services Center.
