School Leader & Staff

Nicolette Whitson, school leader


Mrs. Whitson is completing her fourth year working for The Leona Group and has been at Eagle Academy for three years, serving in the roles of English teacher, administrative support and most recently as instructional coach.

Prior to working for The Leona Group, Mrs. Whitson began her teaching career as an adult ESL teacher for Arabic women whose husbands were international students studying at the University of Toledo. She also served as the assistant principal of academics for St. Ursula Academy in Toledo for two years.

Mrs. Whitson holds a B.A. in adolescent to young adult education with a specialization in English and language arts from Lourdes University.  She also holds a  M.D.E. in education leadership. Additionally, she holds teaching licenses for English grades 6-12 in both Michigan and Ohio. Next month, she will graduate with her masters in educational leadership from Lourdes University and will hold an Ohio Principal's license in grades 5-12.
