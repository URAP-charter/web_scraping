Athletics

Athletic Director: Coach John Jergovich

 

Chandler Park Academy offers an array of athletic teams. Our student athletes are some of the most talented and dedicated that the metro Detroit area has to offer. It is the mission of the athletics department at CPA, to stand firm in the belief that our athletes are students first.

 

If our students do not excel in the classroom, they will not get the opportunity to excel on the field or court. Athletes are required to submit progress reports and attend study sessions weekly.
