Middle School: 6-8

Welcome to Chandler Park Academy Middle School.

 

 

Our Mission at Chandler Park Academy is to deliver academic excellence, character development and leadership for the urban child who would benefit from a culturally and academically enriched K-12 college preparatory education.

 

 Chandler Park Academy Middle School provides a comprehensive program that includes the core subjects and a wide range of elective courses, special programs and activities. You can be assured your child will receive an outstanding education in a safe, supportive and nurturing school environment. With our team of highly qualified and dedicated educators, your child will reach their maximum potential, academically, socially and aesthetically.

 

Thank you in advance for visiting our site.
