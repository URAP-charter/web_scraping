Please mark Saturday, January 30th on your calendars! 8th grade parents and students have prepared several science related exhibits and activities for community families using 4-H STEM (science, technology, engineering, math) curriculum of the Adams County Extension service. It will be hands on and fun for kids and adults alike. There will be six educational stations featuring electric, wind and rocket power experiments and even a station titled, “Are You Smarter Than an 8th Grader,” will be included. See if you can outsmart our 8th grade students! The STEM festival runs from 10am to 2pm. Lunch concessions will be available to purchase. The cost to attend the event is only $5. Please preregister on the sign-up sheet in the office to help the committee know how many participants to expect.
