Supply List

 

Kindergarten Supply List – 2016-2017

  • 1 box Crayola Crayons (24 count)
  • 3 pkg. Crayola Markers, wide tip (8 count)
  • 1 school bag or backpack
  • 1 water bottle that closes
  • 1 crayon box/pencil box
  • 6 glue sticks
  • 1 pink eraser
  • 1 2-pocket folder, any design or color
  • Last name A – M: gallon-size baggies
  • Last name N – Z:  sandwich-size baggies

First Grade Supply List – 2016-2017

  • 12 wooden, six-sided #2 pencils
  • 1 large pencil eraser
  • 1 large pencil box (big enough to hold the crayons, markers, colored pencils, one glue stick and a pair of scissors)
  • 1 box of 10 thick-line, washable, Crayola markers
  • 1 box Crayola colored pencils (12 ct.)
  • 1 box Crayola Crayons (24 ct.)
  • 1 roll of paper towels
  • 3 glue sticks
  • 3 sturdy folders with pockets (1 purple, 1 green, 1 yellow)
  • 1 box of sandwich size baggies
  • 2 large boxes of Kleenex
  • 1 spiral notebook, wide-ruled
  • 1 composition notebook, wide ruled
  • 1 red pen
  • 1 water bottle (with a lid that your child can close completely)
  • 1 backpack
  • 2 packs of index cards
  • 2 black, dry-erase markers
  • 1 pack of addition fact cards (keep these at home to practice)
  • 1 pack of subtraction fact cards (keep these at home to practice)

Second Grade Supply List – 2016-2017

  • 1 large pink eraser or package of cap erasers

Mission

Our mission is to serve student needs and achieve educational excellence. Students will have a positive learning environment that will give them the ability to become life long learners, while encouraging responsibility, citizenship, honesty, integrity and respect between students and staff. Every student will have the opportunity to reach high academic goals within a broad, well-rounded curriculum. Community participation will aid in guiding students to reach their highest academic potential.
