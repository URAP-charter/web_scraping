  • Student Absence Form

  • Please complete this form and drop it by the office. Extra copies are available at the office as well.

    >>> Prearranged Absence Form
    • Please complete two school weeks in advance.
    • Prearranged absences may be Approved or Unapproved.
    • Please remember that students who are absent from class more than 15 times during a semester will not receive credit for that course.
    • This form will be reviewed by the administration and approved or unapproved. Please contact the office for details.
    • Parent is responsible for acquiring all assignments from teachers.
    • Student must complete all work assigned by the teacher during their absence in accordance with the attendance policy in BLRA handbook.
    • If the grading quarter ends before the two week period the student will only receive credit for the work he has completed.

  • Dates of Absence

Future and/or planned absences, please complete a Pre-Arranged Absence Form
