]]> http://www.blracademy.org/class-pictures-are-tomorrow-21/feed/ 0 2995954 Colorado Springs Safety Notice http://www.blracademy.org/colorado-springs-safety-notice/ http://www.blracademy.org/colorado-springs-safety-notice/#respond Fri, 27 Jan 2017 23:17:25 +0000 http://www.blracademy.org/?p=2995950 January 27, 2017

Greetings BLRA families,

Two separate incidents involving attempted abductions of high school aged females have occurred in Colorado Springs this week. The first attempted abduction occurred near Sand Creek Zone schools in District 49. The second occurred yesterday, to the west of District 49, along North Academy Boulevard.

Given the recent events, District 49’s safety and security team encourages parents to talk with their children about possible interactions with strangers. The following resources provide good information to prepare for and begin a dialogue with your child.

National Crime Prevention Council:

http://www.ncpc.org/topics/violent-crime-and-personal-safety/strangers

Parents Magazine:

http://www.parents.com/kids/safety/stranger-safety

Please be assured that our staff will remain vigilant during those times when our learners are arriving at school and departing at the end of the day. If you ever observe anything suspicious around our school, please call the front office immediately.

I appreciate your continued collaboration in maintaining a safe environment for our students, staff and visitors at our school

Contact Dave Watson, District 49 safety and security director, with any community concerns that may impact safe school environments. His email address is dwatson@d49.org.

 

In collaboration,​​​​​​​

Eric Dinnel
