My name is Donny Baca and I am a Child & Youth Behavioral School Counselor with the Military & Family Life Counseling Program (MFLC). I am a Masters-level Licensed Professional Counselor and I provide short-term, non-medical, solution-focused counseling, education and support to military service members and their families. The MFLC program is an additional resource to service members and their families to address the day-to-day stressors of military life and the impact of deployment and reintegration. Issues can include communication, family dynamics, grief and loss, deployment and reintegration, transitions (parental divorce, relocation), social skills and peer interactions. The MFLC services complement, support and augment existing military support services. MFLC counseling is available to all students, family and staff for an initial consultation regardless of Military status.  I will evaluate and assist with any referrals that may be appropriate. With the exception of mandatory state, federal, and military reporting requirements (i.e., domestic violence, child abuse, and duty-to-warn situations), MFLC support is private and confidential to encourage the widest level of participation. Again, there are NO RECORDS kept and no notes are taken.  It is a strictly confidential service for the student’s and families of the school.

Please review the MFLC Parent Information Letter and provide your signature acknowledging that a CYB-MFLC is available to your child and that you authorize and/or not authorize your child to receive CYB-MFLC support and return the Parent Information Letter to your student(s) teacher.

]]> 2995358 High School Expansion Update http://www.blracademy.org/high-school-expansion-update/ Wed, 16 Dec 2015 16:14:48 +0000 http://www.blracademy.org/?p=2995327 BLRA EXPANDING TO HIGH SCHOOL  –  FALL 2017

In a unanimous vote Thursday evening, the Falcon 49 District School Board approved BLRA’s application for expansion to our charter agreement from K-8 to K-12. BLRA will offer seats to freshmen and sophomores in the Fall of 2017. We will add a junior class in Fall 2018, and senior class the following year (Fall 2019).

BLRA will offer a rigorous, college prep, high school experience that is consistent with our top-rated, award-winning K-8 program. The high school program will include extensive Advanced Placement course offerings, daily use of technology, athletics, and more.

Our expansion includes a new, state-of-the-art middle and high school campus that will be built in the Banning Lewis Ranch housing community. Our current campus location will continue to house our elementary grades, and will allow BLRA to open more seats to students in Kindergarten through fifth grade.

We would like to thank our families and entire school community for your interest and support in this project.

Please check back for details about upcoming announcements and information sessions regarding enrollment.
