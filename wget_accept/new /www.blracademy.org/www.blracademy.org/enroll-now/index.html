Once you submit an online application, you will receive a “Parent Enrollment Form Copy” email. This is your confirmation that your application was received. If you do not receive this email after submitting your application, please contact our registrar Vanessa Carlson, at vcarlson@blracademy.org.

*Submitting an application for your child does not mean they are enrolled at BLRA and have a seat. You will be notified when/if a seat is available for your student.

Enrollment at BLRA for Fall 2017 will be awarded by priority status.

Open enrollment for the 2017-2018 year will run from November 1, 2016 through February 28, 2017.  Our lottery will be held on March 16, 2017 at 6pm in the BLRA Library.

Priority status is defined as follows:

  1. Banning Lewis Ranch- Residential Development
  2. Siblings of Students Currently Enrolled in BLRA
  3. Currently Enrolled Authorized Charter School Students
  4. Students who reside within School District 49 Geographical Boundaries
  5. Students who reside outside of School District 49 Geographical Boundaries

Currently enrolled students do not have to go through the lottery process. Once enrolled, a child can remain at BLRA until they choose to withdraw. If you have specific questions regarding the status of your child, please feel free to email our Registrar Vanessa Carlson, at vcarlson@blracademy.org.

Upcoming Informational Meetings will be held at 6pm in the BLRA Library on:

  • November 30, 2016
  • January 12, 2017
  • February 15, 2017

It is expected that you attend one of the informational meetings listed above. Please note child care is not provided, but children are welcome to attend with their parents. This is a time for parents to receive information regarding our program and have the opportunity to ask questions and tour our facility.
