Athletics

Monte del Sol Dragons and Lady Dragons rule !

Athletics Coordinator: Alfredo Lujan 505-670-2339 alujan@montedelsol.org 

We have a strong, competitive and exciting athletics program building on success year-after-year offering soccer, volleyball, basketball and baseball.  We do not offer football.  See list of coaches and directions to home games below. 

If you have any questions, please call  Athletics Coordinator, Alfredo Lujan,  505-670-2339 alujan@montedelsol.org  

Despite not having a home field, our Boys' Soccer team has qualified for State for eight years and were seeded #5 in 2011. Our Girls' Soccer team qualified for State for the first time in 2011.  

Our Varsity Boys' Basketball program has qualified for State four times and has always attracted quality atheletes.  Our Basketball team was first in the District in 03-04, qualified for regionals in 04-05 and state in 05-06.  Go Dragons!  Girls' Basketball program is strong and proactive, poised for success.  Additionally, we consistently build our Middle School and JV programs to prepare our athletes for Varsity play.

Our Volleyball Girls are hardworking and focused. Though they have not yet qualified for post-season play they now believe that they are ready to turn the corner.

Our Baseball team was on an 18-game win streak and ended the season with a 22-3 overall record two years ago.  We are now in a rebuilding phase and working diligently to expand the program.

Athletics are open to all High School students and there is a 'no cut' policy, meaning all students who try out for a team will play to some degree.  For more details and up-to-date game information, contact Athletics Director, Lisa Otero, 982-5225 ext. 103 or cell 231-2415.

As we do not have our own gym nor soccer field, practices and home games take place either at Genoveva Chavez Community Center, Ft. Marcy Complex or the Municipal Recreation Complex (MRC) fields off Hwy. 599 depending on the sport and season.

Prerequisities for Participation in Athletics  

Students interested in participating in athletics at Monte del Sol Charter school should be aware of the following:

  1. You must have a physical. The form is available in the school office and from the Ad. It must be completed after May 1 of the previous school year.
  2. You must have an Emergency Information card filled out and signed by your parents. The form is available in the office.
  3. You must have a parental Consent and Release of Liability form signed by your parents. The form is available in the office.
  4. You must have medical insurance, a requirement that can be satisfied in any of the following ways:
  • you can be covered by a parent's policy
  • you can have access to the Indian Hospital
  • you can purchase an accident policy through the school (if available)
All of the above-mentioned forms must be submitted to the Athletic Director or head coach prior to any practice or competition by the student.

Directions to Athletic Facilities 

Soccer games at Municipal Recreation Complex (MRC), Caja del Rio off Hwy. 599, Santa Fe 

If driving north on I-25 from Albuquerque, Hwy. 599 is the first Santa Fe exit.  Take ramp to #599 and go two miles to Airport Rd.  Cross Airport Rd. (you will still be on #599 ) and continue approx a mile and a half--you'll start seeing signs for the MRC. Turn left across the highway onto Cty. Rd. 62 and follow the well posted signs out to the MRC fields.

If coming from north side of Santa Fe or downtown.  Take St. Francis Dr. north and take ramp to #599.  Go approx 7 miles and turn right at the sign for MRC.  Follow well posted signs out to the MRC fields.

For Basketball home games at Ft. Marcy Recreational Complex, 490 Washington Ave., Santa Fe

From St. Francis Drive (driving north) turn Right on Paseo de Peralta (the turn past Alameda St. not the turn past the railroad tracks)

Left on Washington Ave. Quarter mile, then Left at the Artist Rd. traffic light into the Ft. Marcy Complex

For Basketball home games at Genoveva Chavez Center, 3221 Rodeo Rd, Santa Fe

From St. Francis Dr. (driving South) turn right onto Zia Rd. which will merge with Rodeo Rd. in 1.5 miles.  Quarter mile past the traffic light at Ave. de las Campanas turn Right into the Genoveva Chavez Center driveway.  Note that you cannot turn left from Rodeo into the Chavez Ctr. driveway.
