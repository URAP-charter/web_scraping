DIRECTIONS TO OUR CAMPUS   

Enter 4157 Walking Rain Road, Santa Fe, NM 87507 in your GPS (but watch out, Siri will tell you to turn right at Soaring Eagle -- don't -- we're straight ahead )

 

We are located at 4157 Walking Rain Road in the Nava Ade neighborhood off South Richards Avenue (on the way to the Community College).  We are also easy to reach from I-25. 


From Rodeo Rd.
South on Richards Ave. (left on Richards if coming from Zia/Rodeo).   Go one mile and turn RIGHT at the light at Governor Miles Half mile, turn LEFT on Dancing Ground   Then two blocks and turn LEFT on Walking Rain to end of street.

From I-25
Take the Cerrillos Road exit and go one mile to the traffic light for Jaguar Drive and Governor Miles Rd.  Turn RIGHT on Governor Miles, go half a mile and turn RIGHT on Dancing Ground  Then two blocks turn LEFT on Walking Rain to end of street.
