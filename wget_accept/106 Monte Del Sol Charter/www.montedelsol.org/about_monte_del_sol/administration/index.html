Administration and Staff

 

Dr. A. ROBERT JESSEN, Head Learner arjessen@montedelsol.org 505-982-5225 ext. 101  Dr. Jessen took the helm as Head Learner / Principal at Monte del Sol in 2014   Robert joined the faculty of Monte del Sol in 2009 to teach Spanish following ten years teaching Latin American politics at the College of Santa Fe.  He earned his PhD from Northwestern University studying relations between Tarahumara Indians in Mexico and their Mestizo neighbors. Robert infused his Spanish classes with the Rassias Method he taught as an undergraduate at Dartmouth College and honed as a Spanish/French teacher at a Maine boarding school. He lives in Santa Fe with his Chihuahuenese wife Mirna, and their son, Turis.   

JAMIE SOWERS, Assistant Head Learner / Special Services Coordinator  jsowers@montedelsol.org 505-577-0408   Jaime came to Monte del Sol in 2012 from Delaware to work in our the Special Services Department.  He holds a Bachelor's degree in Behavioral Science and a Master's in Special Education.  Prior to moving to the Southwest he worked with children in many capacities and enjoys the culture of Monte. In his free time, he enjoys traveling with his son, camping, hiking and attending festivals and concerts. 

CARLY NEWFELD, School Secretary  cnewfeld@montedelsol.org 505-982-5225 ext. 100. Carly is a founding faculty member and parent of a Monte del Sol graduate.  She manages the front office and oversees the Admissions Lottery. Carly grew up in England and first worked in education during an internship in London. She was a research assistant with Amnesty International and later headed the outreach program for Buckminster Fuller's visionary Planetary Citizens.  Carly moved to Santa Fe and joined the editorial team of Mothering Magazine. She is the author of two books about the Findhorn Foundation's sustainable community in the Scottish Highlands where she was on staff for nine years. In addition, Carly has produced and hosted radio shows in Santa Fe since 1994. 

ALISON WATT, Registrar, awatt@montedelsol.org  505-982-5225 ext. 104  Alison joined Monte del Sol in 2014 as  is our Registrar. She was born and raised in Southern California where she developed a love of sailing and spent many years working on sailing vessels, fishing vessels and a ferry boat.  She graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science from Western Washington University.  Alison has a love of Charter Schools and prior to working at Monte del Sol she worked at Anansi Charter School in Taos and McCurdy Charter School in Espanola, NM and has also worked as a Charter School start-up consultant.
