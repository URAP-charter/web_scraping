BOARD OF GOVERNORS 2016 to 2017 

board@montedelsol.org



BRETT FRAUENGLASS —   (bfrauenglass@montedelsol.org)
 Brett has undergraduate degrees from St. John's College and the University of New Mexico. After teaching high school in Santa Fe, Pecos and Tokyo, he studied architecture at the Boston Architectural Center and designed schools in the Boston area for ten years. He now works as an architect with the City of Albuquerque.  Brett is also an instructor at Ski Santa Fe and co-director of their White Tornados program for local youth. Brett and his wife, Ondine, have four sons: Clark (MdS Class of 2011), Lyle (two years at MdS, then accepted to NMSA theater, Class of 2014),  Hans (MdS Class of 2017), and Jean (MdS Class of 2019). Brett joined the Governing Board in 2009.




CASSANDRA REID — Board President  
(creid@montedelsol.org) 
Cassandra is a former Monte del Sol PTSA President. She lived in Albuquerque for 17 years before moving to the Santa Fe area in 2005 and is an artist who creates large, handmade tile mosaics in collaboration with communities.  She has exhibited her sculptures in galleries nationally and internationally. Since receiving her MFA from UNM in 1996, she has worked in schools, treatment centers and neighborhoods to create large mosaics with the help of students, parents, grandparents, inmates, teachers and many others. She has also been active in community organizations on issues of education, culture and environmental justice. She enjoys hiking, going to galleries or just goofing around at home with her family of people, horses and cats. Her son graduated with Monte del Sol's Class of 2014 and her stepdaughter graduated with The Class of 2011. Even before she moved to Santa Fe she hoped that her children would one day attend Monte del Sol and she is grateful to be part of this school community.




ANNE SALZMANN (asalzmann@montedelsol.org)
Anne is in her 5th year as Head of School for The MASTERS Program Early College Charter School. She spent 7 years at Monte del Sol Charter School in Santa Fe, six of those years as Assistant Head Learner and one year as Interim Head Learner. Prior to moving to Santa Fe, Anne was Head of Verde Valley School in Sedona, AZ, an international boarding school. Anne has a Master’s Degree in Counseling and Consulting Psychology from Harvard University and has worked in schools all of her life.




  MICHAEL SMITH — Treasurer (mssmith@montedelsol.org)
Michael is married to Iba Fukuda (youngest daughter of Hiro and Shohko Fukuda of Shohko Cafe) and the father of Lia Fukuda, MDS class of 2018 and Emi Fukuda class of 2020 and Taiyo Fukuda class of 2022. Michael and Iba met while attending the Evergreen State College in Olympia, WA and both share a passion for alternative education. Michael works as an Association Manager with HOAMCO and manages 9-10 Condo and HOA Associations in Santa Fe. Iba and Michael owned a restaurant, Citron, in Oakland CA, near Berkeley for 4 years back in the late 90s. Prior to that and Evergreen, Michael was a practitioner of Zen Buddhism at the San Francisco Zen Center. Iba, Michael and the kids have lived in Santa Fe since 2002 and have looked forward to being involved with Monte Del Sol since then. Michael was co-founder and past president of Santa Fe JIN (Japanese Intercultural Network) as well as serving on the Board of the Santa Fe Realtor's Association and Youth Shelters and Family Services.




  RACHEL ZELIZER — Student Representative (rzeli@montedelsol.org)
Rachel Zelizer has been a student at Monte del Sol for 5 years(Class of 2018), and a participant in National Honor Society, Model United Nations, and several other extracurricular activities. She hopes to go on to study and work in international relations.
