 Online Registration begins August 8th

Log in to your PowerSchool Parent Portal Account today, and click on the “Forms” link.

 

Select the instructions to set up your eFunds account. This form will give you the student number you need to enroll in eFunds to pay your Registration fees online.

Log in again on Monday, August 8th to complete the Welcome Back Packet of forms.

Don’t have a PowerSchool Parent Portal Account?  Email cnewfeld@montedelsol.org to request your Parent Portal Instructions.

Don’t remember your PowerSchool username or password? Email awatt@montedelsol.org to request a PASSWORD RESET.

   

2016 Registration Dates for ALL new and returning students !  

Doors open 9:00 a.m. 

 

7th & 8th Grade: Monday, August 15th  9:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. 

8th & 9th Grade: Tuesday, August 16th    9:00 a.m.  -- 1:00 p.m.  

Juniors & Seniors: Wednesday, August 17th  9:00 a.m. -- 1:00 p.m.  

 

If you have students in more than one grade come on the date most convenient to you.

Questions to Alison Watt: awatt@montedelsol.org
