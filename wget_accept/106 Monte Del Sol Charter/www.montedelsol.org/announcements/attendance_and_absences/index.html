Attendance and Absences

To report your child absent please call the Attendance Line by 10:00 a.m.  505-982-5225, press 3.

If you do not call the Attendance Line your child will be marked Unexcused Absent (AU) and you will receive a 'robocall' at the end of the school day to inform you that your child was absent.  So, please call ! 

These procedures are a primary component of school safety and ditching prevention and we cannot stress enough how important it is that you remember to call the Attendance Line.  Thank you !

If you pick up your child for an appointment during the school day please come into the front office to sign the early sign out sheet.  Do NOT ask your child to meet you on the curb.   Thank you!

Please direct questions about absences to Graciela 'Chela' Butler at gbutler@montedelsol.org  505-982-5225 ext. 118
