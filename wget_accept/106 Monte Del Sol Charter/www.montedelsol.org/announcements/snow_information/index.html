Snow Delay Schedule and Information

We follow Santa Fe Public Schools' (SFPS) snow decisions.


If SFPS announce a 2-hour delay, Monte del Sol buses will run two hours later than normal and classes will begin at 10:45 a.m.  If SFPS classes are cancelled, our classes will also be cancelled.  

PLEASE NOTE that sometimes a two-hour delay is changed to a closing due to dangerous road conditions.

If it snows overnight but you see no mention of Santa Fe Public School delays on local TV stations, buses and schools will follow their normal schedules.  Remember, snowfalls vary widely throughout Santa Fe County.  

If you have any questions about your child's school bus time please call Martin Hererra at 505-270-1001

We will text all parents once we know whether school is delayed or closed.  

 

Important note regarding Mentorships on Snow Days

Students are responsible for contacting their Mentor directly to find out if the Mentorship is happening that day or not. Mentorships which use our campus will be cancelled if snow conditions cause the school to be closed or released early. 

It is vital that you and your child keep a copy of the mentor's phone number handy. Please also make sure that your mentor has your home or cell number. The Mentorship Office on campus will be closed on snow days.
