Student Application for Focus Academy

Please complete all fields as indicated and click the "Submit" button at the bottom.  A copy of your student's IEP is also required and can either be faxed to 813.443.5630, emailed to admissions@focusacademytampa.org, or mailed to our school address. We would also like a photo of your student to add to their file.

Thank you for submitting your student application! It will be forwarded to our Admissions Committee for review. Please remember to submit a copy of your student's most recent IEP as well. Once we have received and reviewed all information we will contact you.
