      Focus Academy  in Tampa

      Not every student is alike.  Some students have challenges that make learning in a traditional academic environment frustrating. Recognizing that these students require unique strategies for making learning fresh and accessible is what Focus Academy is all about.  

      High school should prepare young people for making the transition into the adult world.  It is a time of discovering one’s individuality and passions, of deciding on a career or course of further study.  However, many students with these challenges are not fulfilling their potential as traditional schools allow them to fall through the cracks.   

      At Focus Academy, we want to challenge our students to reach their highest level.  We will work with you to determine each student's goal and how to reach it, knowing that each individual is different and their potential is still being discovered.  Focus Academy strives to ensure that students are prepared for future success.  special education in Tampa social skills training in Tampa
