    ​Focus Academy honored at FDLRS awards

    On May 9, Focus Academy received the award for Best School Administration at the 17th Annual Florida Diagnostic and Learning Resources System (FDLRS) Excellence in Action Awards. The Excellence in Action awards recognize individuals who have made a difference in the life of a student with special needs. Parents, caregivers or students receiving Exceptional Student Education (ESE) services through Hillsborough County Public Schools (HCPS) nominate deserving individuals within the educational setting or community groups and volunteers. Read more about the awards and the nominees in the FDLRS newsletter.

    Focus Academy received multiple nominations in Administration, High School ESE Teacher, and Support Staff categories, and received the award for Administration. Many thanks to those parents who submitted nominations! The beautiful award is on our reception desk in the main office; please stop by and admire it!​

    Clinical Director Loretta Gallo-Lopez receives honor

    The Florida Association for Play Therapy recently honored Clinical Director Loretta Gallo-Lopez with the 2014 Viola Brody Award Recipient for Play Therapist of the Year. The award is given "in recognition of outstanding contribution to the field of play therapy." This prestigious award is given to only one person in the state annually. Congratulations, Loretta!​​
