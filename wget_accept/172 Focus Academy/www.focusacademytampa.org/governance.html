    ​​Focus Academy was founded in 2012 by parents and professionals who saw a need for a high school for students with learning challenges that emphasized social connections and relationship building. ​

    Our Governance Board is made up of community members that are committed to making Focus Academy the finest quality school possible.  Meetings are monthly and open to the public.​​
