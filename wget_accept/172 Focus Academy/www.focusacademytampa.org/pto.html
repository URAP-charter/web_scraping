The Focus Academy PTO supports the school through volunteering, fundraising, and other efforts. The main event organized by the PTO is the annual Focus Academy Gala. The PTO also sponsors Parent Education Seminars throughout the year on topics of interest to parents of special needs students. Dad Squad is a group that concentrates on providing a social outlet for parents and students together.
