Somerset Achievements

Congrats Somerset Wrestlers!

Somerset Wrestling concluded their post-season by finishing 4th in the State scoring 89 points and placing 5 wrestlers in the Top 6, (both are school records), including 1 State Champion! 8th grader, Lucas Willis, won Somerset's 6th Career Individual State Championship in 6 seasons.
Artists Selected to Exhibit Work in Boca Raton!

Congratulations to Miguel Romero, Fabiana Gherardi, Lucia Canizares, Anakarina Beckhart, Fabiana Gherardi, Tabitha Cherubin, Pamela Rodriguez, Johannie Beauvais, Emily Rakela, Manuela Mahecha, Abigail Jones, and Larimar Torres.



Their artwork was chosen to be exhibited at the James A. Rutherford Park Community Center in Boca Raton for the month of March
Wrestling Team Breaks School Records - Moving on to States!

The Somerset Academy Wrestling Team broke multiple school records at the Regional Championship and finished Runner-Up with 242-240 points to host Cardinal Gibbons.(240 Points school record Previous 180.5). 11 wrestlers qualified to State! (School Record Previous 8)
Congratulations Musicians!

Somerset Academy band students did a fabulous job at this year's Solo and Ensemble Evaluation. There participated in almost 30 events including solos, duets, trios, quartets and choirs. We are so proud of them!

DECA Gives Back!

Ms. Echeverria and the Somerset DECA Chapter wanted to give back to the community. What better way than to bring a smile to child's face? With the help of the DECA members, toys were collected and later donated to a small underprivileged community.
Varsity Wrestling Team: District Champs!

The Wrestling team won its 4th Consecutive District Title scoring 239.5 and 10 individual District Champs both breaking school records. All 12 boys qualify for the Region Tournament at Cardinal Gibbons on February 24-25th.
Congratulations Night of the Arts Winners!
