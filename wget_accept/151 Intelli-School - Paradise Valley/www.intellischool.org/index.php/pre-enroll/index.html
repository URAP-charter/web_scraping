Pre-Enroll


Thank you for your interest in IntelliSchool. We’re looking forward to meeting you. Remember, we are a public charter high school, not a GED certification program. We only accept prospective students between the ages of 14 and 21 at the time of their enrollment.

Please fill out this short pre-enrollment form. Once submitted, we’ll get in touch to learn more about you.

If the Student is under 18 please provide Parent / Guardian Name and Contact Information otherwise provide the adult Student’s Contact Information.
