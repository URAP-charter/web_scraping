  By New World Prep • September 8, 2014 Uncategorize

Saturday, September 13th

Spanish-Speakers Session:     9:00am – 10:30am

English-Speakers Session:       10:30am – 12:00pm

In the Main School Building, Room S202

 If you were NOT able to attend the Parent Orientation on Wednesday, September 3rd, please join us for this make-up session. If you attended the September 3rd session, please disregard this invite. Parents and guardians are encouraged to bring a friend or family member who is a positive mentor to their child. Parents may send an adult mentor in their place, if they cannot attend for work related reasons. Otherwise at least one parent or guardian is expected to be in attendance.

Students will receive 50 Puma Bucks each time you attend an event. To view the parent calendar please click HERE.

Parent Orientation/Escolar para Padres de Familia

  By New World Prep • September 2, 2014 Uncategorize

Parent Orientation/Escolar para Padres de Familia

Reminders:

  • Your child’s first day of school is Wednesday, September 3rd, from 8:00am – 2:00 pm.
  • There will be NO busing on the first day of school. However all bus schedules will be distributed that day.
  • In place of school lunch on September 3rd, students and staff will be at the Welcome Back BBQ from 12:00 pm – 2:00pm! All parents, guardians and mentors are encouraged to join us!
  • If you have not filled out the School Lunch Form at www.applyforlunch.com, we can help you complete it during the Welcome Back BBQ. Regardless of income, each parent/guardian must complete a School Lunch Form.

Importante:

  • Primer Día de Clases, Miércoles, Septiembre 3, 8:00am – 2:00 pm.
  • No habrá servicio de Bus Escolar el primer día de clases. Los itinerarios de este servicio serán entregados en esta fecha.
  • En lugar del almuerzo escolar, en Septiembre 3, los estudiantes y el personal asistirán a la Barbacoa de Bienvenida de 12:00 pm – 2:00pm! Todos los padres de familia y apoderados están invitados para acompañarnos!
  • Si aún no ha completado el Formulario Almuerzo Escolar en www.applyforlunch.com, estamos a su disposición durante la Barbacoa de Bienvenida. Es un requisito que todos los padres de familia completen este formulario a principio del año escolar.

 

“The Opening Year” Parent Orientation

 Wednesday, September 3rd, 6th Grade Session: 8:30am – 10:00am,  7th & 8th Grade Session: 10:30am – 12:00pm, in the Gym.

 Parents and guardians are encouraged to bring a friend or family member who is a positive mentor to their child. Parents may send an adult mentor in their place, if they cannot attend for work related reasons. Otherwise at least one parent or guardian is expected to be in attendance.

Should you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at 718.705.8990.

 

“Orientación Apertura del Año Escolar para Padres de Familia”

Miércoles, Septiembre 3, Sesión 6to Grado: 8:30am – 10:00am, Sesión 7mo & 8vo Grados: 10:30am – 12:00pm, en la Cafeteria.

Recomendamos a los PP.FF. la asistencia de un amigo o miembro de la familia que influye positivamente a su hijo. Si por razones de trabajo no puede Ud. asistir, envíe a un mentor de la familia en su lugar. Se espera la asistencia de no menos de un padre de familia o apoderado.

Cualquier pregunta o inquietud, comuníquese llamando al 718.705.8990. Hablamos español.
