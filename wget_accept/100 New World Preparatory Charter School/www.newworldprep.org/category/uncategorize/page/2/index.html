Physical Education Contract/Syllabus(7th Grade)

  By Mr. Endress • September 13, 2016 Mr. Endress, Uncategorize

New World Preparatory Charter School
Physical Education Class 2016-17
Mr. Endress
Email: mendress@newworldprep.org
Welcome Back 7th Graders!
I hope you enjoyed your summer! I am excited to once again be your Physical Education Teacher at New World Preparatory Charter School again! Please review the following overview for the school year. In order for every student to have a safe and successful year in Physical Education, you should be aware of the expectations, guidelines, and procedures when you enter and exit the gym. Please read the overview thoroughly WITH YOUR PARENTS, sign, and return it to school. This will count as the first HW assignment for the year. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or concerns. Thank you!

Expectations for the year:
1) Physical Education class requires that you come to class on time, wearing appropriate attire (sweat pants or shorts, NWP T-shirt or sweatshirt and SNEAKERS) every day you come to the gym.

2) Participation in all activities is required unless you have a documented medical condition that prevents you from doing so.
3) Most importantly, you must treat your peers and teachers with RESPECT at all times.
Daily routines:
When entering the gym:
1) Come in quickly and place all of your belongings in a safe area on the stage.
2) Walk the perimeter of the gym until you hear the whistle.
3) When you hear the whistle, stop where you are, and take a knee.
4) When told to do so, go to your assigned floor spot for attendance and take a seat. Once you arrive at your floor spot, it is important to remain quiet during attendance. (Level 1) Remember: the longer it takes to quiet down, the less time there will be for activities.
5) Listen for any announcements and instructions for the warm up and the daily activity. (Level 0)
At the end of class:
1) When you hear the whistle, take a knee.
a. If you have equipment, listen for instructions and return the equipment to the designated area. Proceed to gather your belongings and prepare for dismissal.
b. If you do not have equipment, quickly gather your belongings and prepare for dismissal.
2) Line up by the exit door as soon as you have gathered your belongings.
REMEMBER: THE WHISTLE DOES NOT INDICATE DISMISSAL FROM THE GYM! You must wait for the bell or the verbal cue to leave the gym.
General guidelines for classroom behavior:
– NO electronics of any kind allowed during class. They WILL be confiscated and your parent will need to come to school to retrieve it.
– NO GUM OR FOOD
– You may only remain in the gym during class. There is no leaving, going on stage, or using the bathroom without permission.
– Students are to conduct themselves in a mature and respectful manner. Horseplay, screaming, cursing, hiding others’ belongings, etc. will NOT be tolerated. Students may receive a phone call home or a detention by violating class etiquette. Vandalism/theft of any kind will result in suspension.
– Students are urged to bring a water bottle everyday to school. Cups WILL NOT be distributed
Grading Rationale:
70% Classwork (Preparedness, attendance, participation)
20% Assessments (Assignments, projects)
10% Homework (Contract)

** 3 unprepared marks in any quarter can result in automatic failure of PE class**

In order to receive full credit for each day, you have to be wearing appropriate physical education uniform and sneakers and you must participate in the warm-up AND the lesson/activity for the day. If you are not wearing sneakers, you will be marked unprepared for the day, even if you are otherwise wearing an appropriate physical education uniform. Medical conditions (with note) are the only accepted excuse for not participating in class.

Each day you will receive a grade between 6-10 based on preparedness, effort, and participation. All grades will be averaged together to come up with your final grade for each quarter. Extra credit assignments WILL be available for all.

We will be covering the basic skills (age-appropriate) and rules for the following sports: Volleyball, Basketball and Soccer. There will also be a class tournament for each sport. The last month of school we will be learning many different group games (kickball, punch ball, castle ball, relay races, playground ball

When the weather is nice AND the class earns the right to go, we may go over to Faber park for outdoor activities .(football, whiffle ball, soccer, frisbee, badminton,etc.)
****Remember, Physical Education is NOT recess****
