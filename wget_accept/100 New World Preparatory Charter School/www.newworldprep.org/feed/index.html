Reading and Writing Homework Packet

1/30/17-2/3/17

 

Name: ___________________________________________________________

 

Directions: Follow the reading directions for each night below. Answer the questions for

each day of the week on a separate piece of loose leaf, staple it to this instructional sheet, and hand in the COMPLETED PACKET to your teacher on Friday.

 

Monday: Answer the questions, based on the “Lewis Hine” article, that follow in COMPLETE sentences.

 

  1. What year was Lewis Hine born? What year did he die?
  2. Where was Mr. Hine born?
  3. When did he begin taking photographs? What was he originally doing for work?
  4. Who were Mr. Hine’s first subjects? Where did he first start taking photographs of people?
  5. How many states did he visit while working for the NCLC?
  6. While taking the photographs what does Hine always do?
  7. Was this a necessary act for him to do?

 

 

Tuesday: Complete a Q.O. based on the following topic: Why did Lewis Hine work for the NCLC?

Wednesday: Answer the questions, based on the “International Photography Hall of Fame” article, that follow in COMPLETE sentences.

  1. What happened to Hine early in his life that changed his childhood?
  2. What did Hine do to help his family? How much did he make?
  3. What kind of life did Lewis want for himself?
  4. During his time as a teacher what did he introduce his students to? Why do you think this was important?
  5. What did Hine often do to get access into the factories and other places that children worked?
  6. Why do you think the work that Lewis Hine did was important? Cite evidence from the text.

 

 

Thursday: Complete a Q.O. based on the following topic: How did Lewis Hine’s background influence his work?
